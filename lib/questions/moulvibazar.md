=== MOULVIBAZAR - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Moulvibazar is famous for which beverage?
A) Coffee
B) Tea
C) Wine
D) Juice
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Tea

[TRUE/FALSE] Moulvibazar is one of the major tea-producing districts in Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Lawachara National Park" is located in which upazila?
A) Moulvibazar Sadar
B) Kamalganj
C) Sreemangal
D) Kulaura
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Sreemangal

[MCQ] "Sreemangal" is known as the...
A) Capital of Bangladesh
B) Tea Capital of Bangladesh
C) Port City
D) Industrial Hub
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Tea Capital of Bangladesh

[TRUE/FALSE] Lawachara National Park is home to the endangered Hoolock Gibbon.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Madhabkunda Waterfall" is located in which upazila?
A) Moulvibazar Sadar
B) Barlekha
C) Sreemangal
D) Kulaura
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Barlekha

[MCQ] Madhabkunda is one of the...
A) Smallest waterfalls
B) Largest waterfalls in Bangladesh
C) Man-made structures
D) Rivers
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Largest waterfalls in Bangladesh

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Khasia" (Khasi) indigenous community lives in parts of Moulvibazar.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Manipuri" community is known for which traditional dance?
A) Kathak
B) Manipuri dance
C) Bharatanatyam
D) Western dance
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Manipuri dance

[MCQ] "Hakaluki Haor" is one of the...
A) Smallest wetlands
B) Largest freshwater wetlands in Bangladesh
C) Man-made lakes
D) Rivers
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Largest freshwater wetlands in Bangladesh

[TRUE/FALSE] Hakaluki Haor is an important bird habitat and Ramsar site.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Hum Hum Waterfall" is located in which upazila?
A) Sreemangal
B) Kamalganj
C) Rajnagar
D) Kulaura
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Kamalganj

[MCQ] Which river flows through Moulvibazar?
A) Padma
B) Manu and Dhalai
C) Meghna
D) Jamuna
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Manu and Dhalai

[TRUE/FALSE] Moulvibazar has hilly terrain with tea gardens.

[MCQ] The "Baikka Beel" is a...
A) River
B) Freshwater wetland sanctuary

CORRECT ANSWER: B) Freshwater wetland sanctuary
