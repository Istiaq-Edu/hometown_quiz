=== RANGPUR - RE-CATEGORIZED AND EXPANDED QUIZ ===
Category: Places and History (Minimum 10 Questions)
This category focuses on specific landmarks, geographical features, historical events, and significant figures associated with Rangpur's past and present.

[MCQ] Rangpur is famously known for the historic "Tajhat Palace". It was the residence of a...
A) Mughal Subahdar
B) Hindu Zamindar (Landlord)
C) British Governor
D) Sultan
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Hindu Zamindar (Landlord)
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Carmichael College" in Rangpur is one of the oldest and most prestigious colleges in Bangladesh, established in 1916.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which river flows near Rangpur city?
A) Ghaghat
B) Padma
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Ghaghat (Teesta is also nearby in the division).
[MCQ] "Begum Rokeya", the pioneer of women's education in Bengal, was born in which district of Rangpur division?
A) Rangpur
B) Dinajpur
C) Thakurgaon
D) Kurigram
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Rangpur (Pairaband village).
[MCQ] The "Kantajew Temple" (Kantaji Temple), famous for its terracotta architecture, is located in which district of Rangpur division?
A) Rangpur
B) Dinajpur
C) Nilphamari
D) Gaibandha
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Dinajpur
[TRUE/FALSE] Rangpur was declared a city corporation in 2012.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] The "Ramsagar" is the largest man-made pond in Bangladesh. It is located in...
A) Rangpur
B) Dinajpur
C) Lalmonirhat
D) Panchagarh
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Dinajpur
[MCQ] "Vinnya Jagat" is a popular amusement park and picnic spot located in...
A) Rangpur
B) Bogra
C) Rajshahi
D) Pabna
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Rangpur
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Teesta Barrage" is the largest irrigation project in Bangladesh, located in the Rangpur division (Lalmonirhat/Nilphamari).
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which district in Rangpur division is the northernmost district of Bangladesh?
A) Panchagarh
B) Thakurgaon
C) Dinajpur
D) Lalmonirhat
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Panchagarh
[MCQ] The "Chini Masjid" (Sugar Mosque) is a unique architectural heritage located in...
A) Rangpur City
B) Saidpur (Nilphamari)
C) Dinajpur
D) Kurigram
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Saidpur (Nilphamari)
[TRUE/FALSE] Rangpur was a center of the "Fakir-Sannyasi Rebellion" and "Peasant Rebellion" (Tebhaga Movement).
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Tetulia" in Panchagarh is famous for being a spot from where one can see...
A) The Bay of Bengal
B) The Kanchenjunga (Himalayas)
C) The Sundarbans
D) The Kaptai Lake
CORRECT ANSWER: B) The Kanchenjunga (Himalayas)
[MCQ] "Banglabandha" land port is located in...
A) Panchagarh
B) Lalmonirhat
C) Kurigram
D) Dinajpur
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Panchagarh (Zero point).
[MCQ] The "Town Hall" of Rangpur is a historic building.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True
[MCQ] "Kellaband" mosque is a Mughal era mosque in Rangpur.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True
[MCQ] Which famous writer and poet was born in Kurigram (Rangpur Division)?
A) Syed Shamsul Haque
B) Humayun Ahmed
C) Al Mahmud
D) Shamsur Rahman
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Syed Shamsul Haque
[TRUE/FALSE] "Swapnopuri" is a famous artificial amusement park in Dinajpur.
CORRECT ANSWER: True


Category: Culture and Traditions (Minimum 10 Questions)
This category covers festivals, arts, crafts, specific cultural practices, and aspects that define the unique identity and heritage of Rangpur and its people.

[MCQ] "Bhawaiya" is a famous folk song genre of which region?
A) Sylhet
B) Chittagong
C) Rangpur
D) Barisal
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Rangpur
[TRUE/FALSE] "Shataranji" (traditional carpet) is a famous craft of Rangpur, recognized as a GI product.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Haribhanga" is a famous variety of...
A) Mango
B) Litchi
C) Jackfruit
D) Banana
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Mango
[MCQ] The "Gambhira" dance is to Rajshahi as "______" is to Rangpur (in terms of folk culture, though Bhawaiya is the main song).
A) Jari Gaan
B) Pala Gaan
C) Bhawaiya (Song)
D) Baul
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Bhawaiya (Song)
[TRUE/FALSE] Tobacco cultivation is a major agricultural activity in the Rangpur region.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Sidha" or "Sidhai" is a traditional food practice in rural Rangpur involving...
A) Fermented fish
B) Steamed rice cakes
C) Dried vegetables
D) Puffed rice
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Fermented fish (Shidol/Sidol is common in the north).
[MCQ] The indigenous "Santal" and "Oraon" communities live in parts of Rangpur division. They have a festival called...
A) Karam
B) Sangrai
C) Bizu
D) Wangala
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Karam
[TRUE/FALSE] "Mofiz" is a colloquial term (often derogatory, but historically rooted) used to refer to seasonal workers from the north (Rangpur/Gaibandha) traveling to Dhaka.

[MCQ] Which district in Rangpur division is famous for "Litchi" (along with Rajshahi/Ishwardi)?
A) Dinajpur
B) Kurigram
C) Lalmonirhat
D) Gaibandha

[MCQ] "Noyabad Mosque" in Dinajpur is built in which architectural style?
A) Mughal
B) Sultanate
C) British
D) Modern
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Mughal
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Chilmari" river port in Kurigram was historically famous for trade.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Kataribhog" is a famous aromatic rice variety from...
A) Dinajpur
B) Rangpur
C) Sylhet
D) Barisal
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Dinajpur
[MCQ] "Abbas Uddin Ahmed" was a legendary singer of which genre?
A) Bhawaiya
B) Bhatiali
C) Baul
D) Nazrul Geeti
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Bhawaiya
[MCQ] "Shidol" is a traditional dried fish preparation popular in Rangpur.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

A) True
B) False
