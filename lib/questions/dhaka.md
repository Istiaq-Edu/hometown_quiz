=== DHAKA - RE-CATEGORIZED AND EXPANDED QUIZ ===
Category: Places and History (Minimum 10 Questions)
This category focuses on specific landmarks, geographical features, historical events, and significant figures associated with Dhaka's past and present.
[MCQ] Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh, is located on the banks of which river?
A) Padma
B) Meghna
C) Buriganga
D) Jamuna
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Buriganga
[TRUE/FALSE] Ahsan Manzil, also known as the "Pink Palace," was the residential palace of the Nawabs of Dhaka.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] What is the name of the famous unfinished Mughal fort in Dhaka?
A) Lalbagh Fort
B) Hajiganj Fort
C) Sonakanda Fort
D) Idrakpur Fort
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Lalbagh Fort
[MCQ] Dhaka University, one of Bangladesh's oldest universities, was established in which year?
A) 1905
B) 1921
C) 1947
D) 1971
CORRECT ANSWER: B) 1921
[MCQ] Which of these is the national mosque of Bangladesh, located in Dhaka?
A) Star Mosque
B) Shahi Mosque
C) Sixty Dome Mosque
D) Baitul Mukarram
CORRECT ANSWER: D) Baitul Mukarram
[TRUE/FALSE] Dhaka is located in the Khulna Division of Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: False
[MCQ] Who made Dhaka the capital of Mughal Bengal in 1610 and named it Jahangirnagar?
A) Shaista Khan
B) Islam Khan I
C) Murshid Quli Khan
D) Mir Jumla II
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Islam Khan I
[TRUE/FALSE] The Curzon Hall at Dhaka University was originally intended to be a new governor's palace but was later made part of the university.
CORRECT ANSWER: False (It was originally intended as a town hall, then a library, before being given to the university).
[MCQ] The Language Movement of 1952, a key event in Bangladesh's history, was centered in which city?
A) Chittagong
B) Comilla
C) Kolkata
D) Dhaka
CORRECT ANSWER: D) Dhaka
[TRUE/FALSE] The High Court Building in Dhaka is a notable example of European-Neoclassical architecture combined with Mughal motifs.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[TRUE/FALSE] Sadarghat, located on the Buriganga river, is one of the largest and busiest river ports in the world.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] The "Aparajeyo Bangla" statue at Dhaka University commemorates the...
A) Language Movement of 1952
B) Liberation War of 1971
C) 1969 Mass Uprising
D) Anti-British Movement
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Liberation War of 1971
[MCQ] What is the approximate total area of Dhaka District (not the entire metropolitan area)?
A) ~850 sq km
B) ~1,460 sq km
C) ~2,100 sq km
D) ~3,200 sq km
CORRECT ANSWER: B) ~1,460 sq km
[TRUE/FALSE] The architectural style of the Star Mosque (Tara Masjid) in Dhaka was originally Mughal, but it was significantly altered with Japanese and English porcelain tiles in the early 20th century.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] The historic "Dhaka Race Course" (now Suhrawardy Udyan) was the site of Sheikh Mujibur Rahman's famous 7th March Speech and the surrender of the Pakistani army. In which year did the surrender take place?
A) 1969
B) 1970
C) 1971
D) 1972
CORRECT ANSWER: C) 1971 (specifically December 16)
[TRUE/FALSE] The name "Dhaka" is popularly believed to be derived from the "Dhak" tree, which was once common in the area.
CORRECT ANSWER: True (This is one of the most popular theories).
[MCQ] Who was the Armenian merchant after whom the historic Armanitola area in old Dhaka is named?
A) Nicholas Pogose
B) Arathoon Michael
C) Johannes Aviet
D) The name is not from a person, but from "Armenian Quarter."
CORRECT ANSWER: D) The name is not from a person, but from "Armenian Quarter." (Armanitola literally means "Armenian Quarter")
[TRUE/FALSE] The main terminal of Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport was designed by the famous French architect Paul Andreu.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] The "Dhakeshwari" Temple, from which Dhaka is also theorized to have gotten its name, is dedicated to which Hindu deity?
A) Shiva
B) Vishnu
C) A form of Durga (Adi Shakti)
D) Ganesha
CORRECT ANSWER: C) A form of Durga (Adi Shakti)
[MCQ] Which Mughal Subahdar is credited with constructing the Bara Katra, a grand caravanserai in Old Dhaka?
A) Shaista Khan
B) Islam Khan I
C) Mir Jumla II
D) Prince Azam Shah
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Shaista Khan
[TRUE/FALSE] The Armenian Church of the Holy Resurrection in Dhaka is one of the oldest churches in Bangladesh, dating back to the 18th century.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] The area known as "Shahbagh" in Dhaka was historically famous for what type of garden or orchard?
A) Mango orchards
B) Rose gardens
C) Spice plantations
D) Tea gardens
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rose gardens


Category: Culture and Traditions (Minimum 10 Questions)
This category covers festivals, arts, crafts, specific cultural practices, and aspects that define the unique identity and heritage of Dhaka and its people.



[TRUE/FALSE] The "Jamdani" sari, a UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage, is a famous product originating from the Dhaka region.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] The "Pohela Boishakh" (Bengali New Year) celebration at Ramna Batamul in Dhaka is organized by which cultural institution?
A) Bangla Academy
B) Chhayanaut
C) Shilpakala Academy
D) Nazrul Institute
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Chhayanaut
[MCQ] Which of these famous delicacies is Dhaka particularly known for?
A) Shatkora Beef
B) Chui Jhal
C) Kacchi Biryani
D) Mezban
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Kacchi Biryani
[MCQ] What is the traditional folk theatre form that is often seen in rural areas surrounding Dhaka, featuring masked performances and mythical stories?
A) Jatra
B) Pala Gaan
C) Lalon Giti
D) Kirtan
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Jatra
[TRUE/FALSE] The annual Dhaka Lit Fest is an international literary festival that brings together writers and thinkers from around the world.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which musical instrument is central to traditional Bengali folk music, often played by street musicians in Dhaka, and has a distinctive gourde body?
A) Tabla
B) Sitar
C) Dotara
D) Ektara
CORRECT ANSWER: D) Ektara
[TRUE/FALSE] Eid-ul-Fitr and Eid-ul-Adha are the two largest religious festivals celebrated with great fervor in Dhaka.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] What is the traditional art of painting rickshaw vans in Dhaka called, known for its vibrant colors and intricate designs?
A) Alpona
B) Nakshi Kantha
C) Rickshaw Art
D) Pot Shilpa
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Rickshaw Art
[TRUE/FALSE] Pitha Utsab, a festival dedicated to traditional Bengali cakes and pastries, is a popular cultural event in Dhaka during winter.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] The Bangla Academy in Dhaka plays a significant role in promoting which aspect of Bengali culture?
A) Traditional dance
B) Bengali language and literature
C) Folk music
D) Culinary arts
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bengali language and literature
[MCQ] What is the significance of the Central Shaheed Minar (Martyr Monument) in Dhaka?
A) It commemorates the Liberation War of 1971.
B) It commemorates the heroes of the Language Movement of 1952.
C) It is a monument to the first capital of Bengal.
D) It celebrates Bengali New Year.
CORRECT ANSWER: B) It commemorates the heroes of the Language Movement of 1952.
[TRUE/FALSE] Traditional Bengali wedding ceremonies in Dhaka often involve elaborate rituals and vibrant processions.
CORRECT ANSWER: True


Category: Everyday Bangladesh (Dhaka Focus) (Minimum 10 Questions)
This category focuses on the practical, social, and logistical aspects of daily life in Dhaka, including transport, demographics, administration, and modern infrastructure.


[MCQ] What is the common name for the three-wheeled passenger carts that are a famous symbol of Dhaka?
A) Tanga
B) Tomtom
C) Rickshaw
D) Auto
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Rickshaw
[MCQ] What major international cricket stadium is located in the Mirpur area of Dhaka?
A) Eden Gardens
B) Sher-e-Bangla National Cricket Stadium
C) M. A. Aziz Stadium
D) National Stadium, Karachi
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sher-e-Bangla National Cricket Stadium
[MCQ] New Market is a well-known...
A) Historical fort
B) Shopping complex
C) River port
D) Public park
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Shopping complex
[MCQ] The Dhaka Metro Rail, a major infrastructure project, began its first phase of commercial operations in which year?
A) 2019
B) 2021
C) 2022
D) 2024
CORRECT ANSWER: C) 2022
[MCQ] How many upazilas (sub-districts) are there within Dhaka District (as of 2025)?
A) 5
B) 10
C) 15
D) 20
CORRECT ANSWER: A) 5 (Dhamrai, Dohar, Keraniganj, Nawabganj, Savar)
[MCQ] According to 2024-2025 estimates, Dhaka consistently ranks as one of the world's...
A) Most livable cities
B) Most densely populated cities
C) Safest cities
D) Cleanest cities
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Most densely populated cities
[MCQ] The literacy rate in Dhaka District, according to the 2022 census, was the highest in Bangladesh, at approximately...
A) 65.2%
B) 72.8%
C) 78.1%
D) 85.4%
CORRECT ANSWER: C) 78.1%
[MCQ] Which of these is NOT one of the 5 upazilas of Dhaka District?
A) Dhamrai
B) Savar
C) Keraniganj
D) Tejgaon
CORRECT ANSWER: D) Tejgaon (Tejgaon is a thana within the City Corporation, not a rural upazila of the district).
[MCQ] What is the primary public transportation system, besides rickshaws and buses, that has recently seen significant expansion in Dhaka?
A) Tram
B) Ferry Service
C) Metro Rail
D) Cable Car
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Metro Rail
[TRUE/FALSE] Traffic congestion is a significant daily challenge for residents and commuters in Dhaka.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which of these is a popular destination for families and young people in Dhaka looking for recreation and green space?
A) Ramna Park
B) Gulshan Lake Park
C) Lalbagh Garden
D) All of the above
CORRECT ANSWER: D) All of the above
[TRUE/FALSE] Dhaka is the financial and commercial hub of Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] What type of market is "Karwan Bazar" famously known for in Dhaka?
A) Electronics market
B) Wholesale raw produce market
C) Textile market
D) Book market
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Wholesale raw produce market
[TRUE/FALSE] Despite being a bustling metropolis, many residents in Dhaka still rely on traditional markets for their daily groceries.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which authority is responsible for urban planning and development in the greater Dhaka area?
A) Dhaka City Corporation North
B) Dhaka City Corporation South
C) Rajdhani Unnayan Kartripakkha (RAJUK)
