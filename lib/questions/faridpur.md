=== FARIDPUR - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Faridpur is named after...
A) A Mughal emperor
B) Sufi Saint Shah Fariduddin
C) A British officer
D) A local zamindar
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sufi Saint Shah Fariduddin

[TRUE/FALSE] Faridpur has a rich history associated with Sufi saints.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river flows through Faridpur district?
A) Jamuna
B) Padma and Kumar
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Padma and Kumar

[MCQ] The "Mirkadim" area is known for which historic structure?
A) Temple
B) Historic bridge (Mirkadim Bridge)
C) Fort
D) Palace
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Historic bridge (Mirkadim Bridge)

[TRUE/FALSE] Faridpur was an important river trading center during British rule.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Madhab Kunda" is a...
A) River
B) Historic pond and pilgrimage site
C) Mountain
D) Forest
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Historic pond and pilgrimage site

[MCQ] Which famous politician was from Faridpur/greater Faridpur region?
A) Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman (from Gopalganj, former Faridpur)
B) Ziaur Rahman
C) Ershad
D) Khaleda Zia
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman

[TRUE/FALSE] Gopalganj was part of greater Faridpur district before becoming a separate district.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Durgapur" upazila is known for...
A) Hills
B) Agricultural land
C) Tea gardens
D) Mining
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural land

[MCQ] Faridpur was historically part of which region?
A) Vanga
B) Pundravardhana
C) Samatata
D) Greater Bengal
CORRECT ANSWER: D) Greater Bengal

[TRUE/FALSE] The Padma River is a major waterway for Faridpur.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Goalchamot" is a famous historic...
A) Temple
B) Mosque
C) Mazar (shrine)
D) Fort
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Mazar (shrine)

[MCQ] "Bhanga" upazila is known for...
A) Hills
B) River transport and agriculture
C) Tea gardens
D) Mining
CORRECT ANSWER: B) River transport and agriculture

[TRUE/FALSE] Faridpur has several historic mosques and shrines.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Kumar River" is significant for...
A) Hydroelectric power
B) Local transport and fishing
C) Gold mining
D) Tourism only
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Local transport and fishing

[MCQ] Which colonial-era institution was established in Faridpur?
A) University
B) Rajendra College (one of the oldest colleges)
C) Airport
D) Port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rajendra College
