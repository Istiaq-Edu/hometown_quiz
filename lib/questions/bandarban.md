=== BANDARBAN - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Bandarban is located in which region of Bangladesh?
A) Barind Tract
B) Chittagong Hill Tracts (CHT)
C) Sylhet Division
D) Sundarbans
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Chittagong Hill Tracts (CHT)

[TRUE/FALSE] Bandarban is known for having the highest peaks in Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Keokradong" is...
A) A river
B) One of the highest peaks in Bangladesh
C) A lake
D) A valley
CORRECT ANSWER: B) One of the highest peaks in Bangladesh

[MCQ] "Tazing Dong" (also known as Bijoy) is claimed to be...
A) The deepest lake
B) The highest peak in Bangladesh
C) A waterfall
D) A temple
CORRECT ANSWER: B) The highest peak in Bangladesh

[TRUE/FALSE] The exact height of the highest peaks in Bandarban is debated.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Nilgiri" is a famous...
A) River
B) Hill resort and tourist spot
C) Temple
D) Market
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Hill resort and tourist spot

[MCQ] Which indigenous community is prominent in Bandarban?
A) Chakma only
B) Marma, Mro, Bawm, Tripura, and others
C) Bengali only
D) Garo
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Marma, Mro, Bawm, Tripura, and others

[TRUE/FALSE] Bandarban has a diverse population of indigenous communities.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Golden Temple" (Buddha Dhatu Jadi) is located in...
A) Chittagong
B) Bandarban
C) Rangamati
D) Khagrachari
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bandarban

[MCQ] The "Nafakhum Waterfall" is located in which upazila?
A) Bandarban Sadar
B) Thanchi
C) Rowangchhari
D) Ruma
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Thanchi

[TRUE/FALSE] Nafakhum is one of the largest waterfalls in Bangladesh by volume.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Boga Lake" is a natural lake located in...
A) Bandarban Sadar
B) Ruma
C) Thanchi
D) Rowangchhari
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Ruma

[MCQ] The "Chimbuk" is a famous...
A) River
B) Hill and viewpoint
C) Temple
D) Market
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Hill and viewpoint

[TRUE/FALSE] Bandarban is the most remote district in the Chittagong Hill Tracts.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Sangu River" flows through Bandarban.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

B) Sangrai (Water Festival)

CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sangrai (Water Festival)

[TRUE/FALSE] "Jhum" cultivation (shifting cultivation) is practiced in Bandarban.

[TRUE/FALSE] Weaving is a traditional craft among women in Bandarban.

[MCQ] Traditional houses in remote areas are often built with...
A) Concrete only
