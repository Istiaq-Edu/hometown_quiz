=== KUSHTIA - RE-CATEGORIZED AND EXPANDED QUIZ ===

Category: Places & History (Minimum 15 Questions)
Historical sites, landmarks, and the rich heritage of Kushtia.

[MCQ] Kushtia is famous as the hometown of which legendary poet and mystic?
A) Rabindranath Tagore
B) Lalon Shah (Fakir Lalon Shah)
C) Kazi Nazrul Islam
D) Michael Madhusudan Dutt
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Lalon Shah (Fakir Lalon Shah)

[TRUE/FALSE] Lalon Shah's mazar (shrine) is located in Cheuria, Kushtia.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Lalon Akhra" (Lalon's spiritual center) is a major...
A) Temple
B) Pilgrimage site for Baul followers
C) Mosque
D) Church
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Pilgrimage site for Baul followers

[MCQ] Kushtia is also associated with which Nobel laureate who had a residence here?
A) Amartya Sen
B) Rabindranath Tagore (Shilaidaha Kuthibari)
C) Muhammad Yunus
D) None
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rabindranath Tagore (Shilaidaha Kuthibari)

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Shilaidaha Kuthibari" was where Rabindranath Tagore wrote many of his famous works.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river is closely associated with Kushtia and Tagore's poetry?
A) Meghna
B) Padma (River Padma)
C) Jamuna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Padma (River Padma)

[MCQ] The "Gorai River" flows through Kushtia and is a distributary of...
A) Jamuna
B) Padma
C) Meghna
D) Brahmaputra
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Padma

[TRUE/FALSE] Kushtia was part of the Nadia district during British rule.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Mohini Mills" was a famous...
A) Rice mill
B) Jute mill and textile factory
C) Sugar mill
D) Paper mill
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Jute mill and textile factory

[MCQ] The "Mir Mosharraf Hossain" memorial is located in Kushtia, honoring the author of...
A) Gitanjali
B) Bishad Sindhu (Ocean of Sorrow)
C) Meghnad Badh Kavya
D) Sanchayita
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bishad Sindhu (Ocean of Sorrow)

[TRUE/FALSE] Mir Mosharraf Hossain is considered a pioneer of Bengali Muslim literature.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Hardinge Bridge" (now railway bridge) connects Kushtia to...
A) Dhaka
B) Pabna (across the Padma)
C) Rajshahi
D) Jessore
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Pabna (across the Padma)

[MCQ] The historic "Lalon Shah Bridge" is located over...
A) Padma River
B) Gorai River
C) Jamuna River
D) Meghna River
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Gorai River

[TRUE/FALSE] Kushtia has a rich history associated with the Indigo Revolt (Nil Bidroha).
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Kumarkhali" upazila is famous for...
A) Mir Mosharraf Hossain's birthplace
B) Hills
C) Tea gardens
D) Fishing
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Mir Mosharraf Hossain's birthplace

[MCQ] The "Pabna-Kushtia region" was historically known for...
A) Hills
B) Fertile agricultural land
C) Mining
D) Forests
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Fertile agricultural land

Category: Culture & Traditions (Minimum 15 Questions)
Local customs, festivals, food, and the cultural identity of Kushtia.

[MCQ] The "Lalon Mela" (Lalon Festival) is held annually on...
A) Eid
B) The death anniversary of Lalon Shah (Dol Purnima)
C) Independence Day
D) Victory Day
CORRECT ANSWER: B) The death anniversary of Lalon Shah (Dol Purnima)

[TRUE/FALSE] The Lalon Mela attracts Baul singers and followers from all over Bangladesh and beyond.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Baul" music and philosophy originated from which region?
A) Chittagong
B) Kushtia-Nadia region (Bengal)
C) Sylhet
D) Rangpur
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Kushtia-Nadia region (Bengal)

[MCQ] Lalon Shah's songs (Lalon Geeti) are known for their themes of...
A) War
B) Humanism, spirituality, and social criticism
C) Comedy
D) Politics only
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Humanism, spirituality, and social criticism

[TRUE/FALSE] Lalon Shah advocated against caste and religious discrimination.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which sweet is Kushtia famous for?
A) Rosogolla
B) Tilersheni and various sweets
C) Chomchom
D) Monda
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Tilersheni and various sweets

[MCQ] "Sugarcane" is a major crop in Kushtia due to...
A) Hilly terrain
B) Fertile alluvial soil
C) Desert climate
D) Coastal location
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Fertile alluvial soil

[TRUE/FALSE] Kushtia is known for its sugar mills (historically).
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Dotara" and "Ektara" are musical instruments associated with...
A) Classical music
B) Baul music
C) Western music
D) Rock music
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Baul music

[MCQ] Which language/dialect is spoken in Kushtia?
A) Sylheti
B) Standard Bengali with local accent

D) Rangpuri
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Standard Bengali with local accent

[TRUE/FALSE] Jute was historically an important crop in Kushtia.

[MCQ] The "Akhra" culture in Kushtia refers to...
A) Temples
B) Spiritual gathering places for Bauls
C) Mosques

[MCQ] "Pitha" (rice cakes) are popular in Kushtia especially during...
A) Summer
B) Winter and harvest festivals

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Urus" (annual fair) at Lalon's shrine is a major cultural event.

[MCQ] Which art form is influenced by Lalon's philosophy?
