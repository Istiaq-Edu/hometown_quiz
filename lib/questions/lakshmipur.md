=== LAKSHMIPUR - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Lakshmipur is located in which administrative division?
A) Dhaka
B) Chittagong
C) Barisal
D) Khulna
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Chittagong

[TRUE/FALSE] Lakshmipur was formerly a mahakuma of Noakhali district.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which major river flows along Lakshmipur?
A) Padma
B) Meghna
C) Jamuna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Meghna

[MCQ] Lakshmipur is known for producing which crop abundantly?
A) Tea
B) Soybeans and Coconuts
C) Rubber
D) Coffee
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Soybeans and Coconuts

[TRUE/FALSE] Lakshmipur is often called the "Soybean Capital" of Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Dalal Bazar" is famous for...
A) Slave trade (historically)
B) Historic Dalal Bazar Zamindar Bari
C) Stock exchange
D) Fish market
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Historic Dalal Bazar Zamindar Bari

[MCQ] "Rambati" or "Ramgati" is an upazila known for...
A) Mountains
B) Riverbank erosion and fishing
C) Tea gardens
D) Desert
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Riverbank erosion and fishing

[TRUE/FALSE] "Alexander" is a famous spot on the Meghna river bank in Lakshmipur.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Kamankhola Zamindar Bari" is historic site.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] Lakshmipur is a coastal district.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] Lakshmipur is famous for which food item?
A) Buffalo Curd (Mohisher Doi) and Hilsa fish
B) Rosogolla
C) Bakarkhani
D) Seven-layer tea
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Buffalo Curd and Hilsa fish

[TRUE/FALSE] Betel nut (Supari) is a major cash crop in Lakshmipur.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Hilsa" (Ilish) fishing in the Meghna river is a major economic activity.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] The dialect of Lakshmipur is similar to...
A) Sylhet
B) Noakhali
C) Barisal
D) Rangpur
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Noakhali

[TRUE/FALSE] Coconut trees are very common in every household.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Everyday Bangladesh

[MCQ] The distance from Dhaka to Lakshmipur is approximately...
A) 100 km
B) 180 km (via Comilla/Noakhali) or shorter via ferry
C) 300 km
D) 400 km
CORRECT ANSWER: B) 180 km

[MCQ] "Majuchoudhurir Hat" is a famous...
A) Ferry ghat connecting Bhola and Barisal
B) Airport
C) Train station
D) Bus terminal
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Ferry ghat

[TRUE/FALSE] There is direct road connectivity to Dhaka.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The main economic activities in Lakshmipur include...
A) IT only
B) Agriculture (Soybean, Coconut), fishing, and remittance
C) Mining
D) Tourism only
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agriculture, fishing, and remittance

[TRUE/FALSE] Lakshmipur has seen development in roads and infrastructure recently.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
