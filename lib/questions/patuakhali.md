=== PATUAKHALI - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Patuakhali is home to which famous sea beach?
A) Cox's Bazar
B) Kuakata
C) Patenga
D) Inani
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Kuakata

[TRUE/FALSE] Kuakata is known as the "Daughter of the Sea" (Sagar Kanya).
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Kuakata is unique because...
A) It has pink sand
B) One can watch both sunrise and sunset from the same beach
C) It has no waves
D) It is an artificial beach
CORRECT ANSWER: B) One can watch both sunrise and sunset

[MCQ] Patuakhali is located in which administrative division?
A) Khulna
B) Barisal
C) Chittagong
D) Dhaka
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Barisal

[TRUE/FALSE] Patuakhali is a coastal district.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river flows near Patuakhali?
A) Padma
B) Payra, Laukathi, and Tentulia
C) Jamuna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Payra, Laukathi, and Tentulia

[MCQ] The "Payra Sea Port" is located in...
A) Chittagong
B) Patuakhali
C) Khulna
D) Cox's Bazar
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Patuakhali

[TRUE/FALSE] The Payra Port is the third sea port of Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Galachipa" and "Kalapara" are upazilas of...
A) Bhola
B) Patuakhali
C) Barguna
D) Pirojpur
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Patuakhali

[MCQ] There is a Rakhine community market in Kuakata.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] The Rakhine people have lived in the region for centuries.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] Patuakhali is famous for which cultural element?
A) Rakhine lifestyle and Buddhist temples in Kuakata
B) Tea gardens
C) Manipuri dance
D) Garo culture
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Rakhine lifestyle and Buddhist temples

[TRUE/FALSE] Dry fish (Shutki) is a common product of the coastal area.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Hilsa" fishing is a major livelihood.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] The "Misripara Buddhist Temple" has a large...
A) Golden Buddha statue
B) Diamond
C) Bell
D) Tree
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Golden Buddha statue

[TRUE/FALSE] Patuakhali has diverse religious heritage including Hindu festivals like Rash Mela.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Everyday Bangladesh

[MCQ] The distance from Dhaka to Patuakhali is approximately...
A) 200 km
B) 320 km

D) 550 km

[MCQ] The Padma Bridge and Lebukhali Bridge (Payra Bridge) have improved road connectivity.
