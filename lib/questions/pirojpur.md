=== PIROJPUR - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Pirojpur is located in which administrative division?
A) Rajshahi
B) Barisal
C) Dhaka
D) Khulna
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Barisal

[TRUE/FALSE] Pirojpur is part of the Barisal (now Barishal) region known for rivers.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river flows through Pirojpur?
A) Padma
B) Baleshwari and Kacha
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Baleshwari and Kacha

[MCQ] Pirojpur is known for producing which fruit?
A) Mango
B) Guava (Peyara)
C) Jackfruit
D) Litchi
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Guava (Peyara)

[TRUE/FALSE] Pirojpur is famous for its guavas, especially from Swarupkathi.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Swarupkathi" upazila is known for...
A) Mountains
B) Guava orchards and floating markets
C) Desert
D) Tea gardens
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Guava orchards and floating markets

[MCQ] The "floating guava market" is a unique feature of Pirojpur.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Pirojpur has a tradition of floating markets (Bazar).
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Mathbaria" upazila is known for...
A) Hills
B) Sundarbans adjacency
C) Tea gardens
D) Airport
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sundarbans adjacency

[MCQ] Pirojpur has access to the Sundarbans.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Rivers and waterways are the lifelines of Pirojpur.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Nazirpur" upazila is known for...
A) Mountains
B) Agricultural production
C) Desert
D) Beach
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural production

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] Pirojpur is known for which product?
A) Tea
B) Guava and other fruits
C) Coffee
D) Rubber
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Guava and other fruits

[TRUE/FALSE] The guava of Pirojpur is exported to other parts of Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Pitha" and sweets in Pirojpur are popular during...
A) Summer
B) Winter
C) Monsoon
D) Autumn
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Winter

[MCQ] The main occupation in Pirojpur includes...
A) IT
B) Agriculture, fruit cultivation, and fishing
C) Mining
D) Heavy industry
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agriculture, fruit cultivation, and fishing

[TRUE/FALSE] Boat is the primary mode of transport in many parts of Pirojpur.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Traditional boat racing is popular in Pirojpur.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Folk songs and Bhatiali music are part of Pirojpur's culture.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Everyday Bangladesh

[MCQ] The distance from Dhaka to Pirojpur is approximately...
A) 200 km
B) 250 km
C) 300 km
D) 350 km
CORRECT ANSWER: B) 250 km

[MCQ] Pirojpur has connectivity via which routes?
A) Only road
B) Road and waterways
C) Only air
D) Only rail
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Road and waterways

[TRUE/FALSE] The Padma Bridge has improved connectivity to Pirojpur from Dhaka.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The main economic activities in Pirojpur include...
A) IT only
B) Agriculture, fruit cultivation, and fishing
C) Heavy industry
D) Gold mining
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agriculture, fruit cultivation, and fishing

[MCQ] Pirojpur's economy is significantly based on...
A) Heavy industry
B) Agriculture and fruits
C) IT
D) Mining
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agriculture and fruits

[TRUE/FALSE] Pirojpur is developing with improved road connectivity via Padma Bridge.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
