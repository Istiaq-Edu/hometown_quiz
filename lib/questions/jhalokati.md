=== JHALOKATI - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Jhalokati is located in which administrative division?
A) Khulna
B) Barisal
C) Dhaka
D) Chittagong
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Barisal

[TRUE/FALSE] Jhalokati was formerly a subdivision of Barisal district.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Jhalokati is famously known as the...
A) "Venice of Bengal" (often applied to Barisal region generally) or "Second Kolkata" (historically)
B) "City of Mosques"
C) "City of Silk"
D) "Desert City"
CORRECT ANSWER: A) "Second Kolkata" (historically)

[MCQ] Which rivers flow through Jhalokati?
A) Padma
B) Sugandha, Bishkhali, and Dhansiri
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sugandha, Bishkhali, and Dhansiri

[MCQ] The "Dhansiri" river is famous because of...
A) Jibanananda Das's poems
B) Large dams
C) Gold deposits
D) High waves
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Jibanananda Das's poems

[TRUE/FALSE] Jhalokati is famous for its floating guava markets.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The floating guava market at "Bhimruli" is located in...
A) Pirojpur/Jhalokati border area
B) Dhaka
C) Sylhet
D) Rangpur
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Pirojpur/Jhalokati border area

[MCQ] "Sujabad Kella" (Fort) is a historic site in Jhalokati.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] Gabkhan Bridge is known as the...
A) Longest bridge
B) Tallest bridge (highest vertical clearance)
C) Wooden bridge
D) Floating bridge
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Tallest bridge

[TRUE/FALSE] The Gabkhan Channel connects Mongla port with Dhaka.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] Jhalokati is famous for which fruit?
A) Mango
B) Guava (Peyara) and Hog Plum (Amra)
C) Jackfruit
D) Litchi
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Guava and Hog Plum

[TRUE/FALSE] Cool mats (Shital Pati) are a traditional product of Jhalokati.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Kirtipasha Zamindar Bari" is a historic palace.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] The main occupation in rural Jhalokati is...
A) IT
B) Agriculture and fishing
C) Mining
D) Heavy industry
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agriculture and fishing

[TRUE/FALSE] Boat is a common mode of transport.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Everyday Bangladesh

[MCQ] The distance from Dhaka to Jhalokati is approximately...
A) 200 km
B) 270 km (by road via Padma Bridge)
C) 400 km
D) 500 km
CORRECT ANSWER: B) 270 km

[MCQ] Traveling by Launch (steamer) to Jhalokati is popular.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] Jhalokati is known for which local sweet?
A) Rosogolla
B) Sandesh
C) Rasmalai
D) Atghar Kuriana's sweets
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sandesh

[MCQ] The main economic activities in Jhalokati include...
A) IT only
B) Agriculture, fruit cultivation, and river trade
C) Heavy industry
D) Gold mining
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agriculture, fruit cultivation, and river trade

[TRUE/FALSE] The Padma Bridge has reduced travel time to Jhalokati.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
