=== GAIBANDHA - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Gaibandha is located in which administrative division?
A) Rajshahi
B) Rangpur
C) Dhaka
D) Sylhet
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rangpur

[TRUE/FALSE] Gaibandha was earlier part of Rangpur district.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which major river flows along the eastern side of Gaibandha?
A) Jamuna (Brahmaputra)
B) Padma
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Jamuna (Brahmaputra)

[MCQ] Gaibandha is known as the "District of..." (folklore).
A) 60,000 Cows (Gai = Cow, Bandha = Tied)
B) 60,000 Horses
C) 60,000 Elephants
D) 60,000 Tigers
CORRECT ANSWER: A) 60,000 Cows

[TRUE/FALSE] The name Gaibandha is believed to come from the fable of King Virat's cow shed.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Balasi Ghat" is a famous...
A) Train ferry ghat (historic) and river scenic spot
B) Airport
C) Tea garden
D) Mountain
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Train ferry ghat and river scenic spot

[MCQ] "Mymensingh-Gaibandha" ferry service operates from Balasi to Bahadurabad (seasonally/historically).
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] "Dreamland" is a recreational park in Gaibandha.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Gaibandha has many "Char" areas (river islands).
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Naldanga Zamindar Bari" is a historic site.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True
