=== MANIKGANJ - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Manikganj is located in which administrative division?
A) Rajshahi
B) Dhaka
C) Khulna
D) Mymensingh
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Dhaka

[TRUE/FALSE] Manikganj is known for its proximity to Dhaka.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river flows through Manikganj?
A) Padma
B) Jamuna and Dhaleshwari
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Jamuna and Dhaleshwari

[MCQ] "Aricha" is famous for its...
A) Temple
B) Ferry ghat (river port) to Paturia
C) Airport
D) Hills
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Ferry ghat

[TRUE/FALSE] The Aricha-Paturia ferry crossing connects Manikganj to the north-western region.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Jamuna River" forms which boundary of Manikganj?
A) Eastern
B) Western
C) Southern
D) Northern
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Western

[MCQ] "Baliati Zamindar Bari" (Palace) is a historic...
A) Temple
B) Zamindar palace complex
C) Mosque
D) Fort
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Zamindar palace complex

[TRUE/FALSE] Baliati Palace is one of the largest zamindar complexes in Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Saturia" upazila is known for...
A) Mountains
B) Agricultural production
C) Desert
D) Beach
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural production

[MCQ] Manikganj was historically part of...
A) Chittagong
B) Greater Dhaka region
C) Sylhet
D) Rangpur
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Greater Dhaka region

[TRUE/FALSE] Manikganj has fertile agricultural land due to riverine alluvial deposits.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Teota Zamindar Bari" is another historic palace in...
A) Manikganj
B) Dhaka
C) Rangpur
D) Sylhet
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Manikganj

[MCQ] "Ghior" upazila is known for...
A) Hills
B) Agricultural areas
C) Tea gardens
D) Sea port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural areas

[TRUE/FALSE] Several historic zamindar houses are found in Manikganj.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
