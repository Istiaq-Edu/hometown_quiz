=== NATORE - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Natore was the headquarters of...
A) Mughal Empire
B) Natore Rajbari (Queen Bhabani's Estate)
C) British East India Company
D) Portuguese traders
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Natore Rajbari

[TRUE/FALSE] "Natore Rajbari" is a major tourist attraction with beautiful palaces.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Uttara Ganabhaban" (Northern People's House) is located in Natore.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] Uttara Ganabhaban was formerly known as...
A) Dighapatia Rajbari
B) Taj Mahal
C) Ahsan Manzil
D) Rose Garden
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Dighapatia Rajbari

[TRUE/FALSE] It serves as the official residence of the Head of Government in the North.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Natore is located in which administrative division?
A) Rangpur
B) Rajshahi
C) Dhaka
D) Khulna
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rajshahi

[MCQ] "Chalan Beel", the largest wetland, covers parts of Natore.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] "Singra" upazila is part of the Chalan Beel area.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river flows near Natore?
A) Padma (at boundary) and Atrai/Nandakuja
B) Meghna
C) Surma
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Padma and Atrai/Nandakuja

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] Natore is famously known for which sweet?
A) Kachagolla of Natore
B) Rosogolla
C) Chomchom
D) Monda
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Kachagolla of Natore

[TRUE/FALSE] Kachagolla is a dry sweet made from milk curd.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Queen Bhabani was a legendary philanthropist and ruler of Natore.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] Agriculture is the main occupation.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Fishing in Chalan Beel is a major seasonal activity.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Everyday Bangladesh

[MCQ] The distance from Dhaka to Natore is approximately...
A) 200 km
B) 250 km
C) 350 km
D) 450 km
CORRECT ANSWER: B) 250 km

[MCQ] Natore is a major railway junction connecting North and South Bengal.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True (Abdulpur Junction nearby, Natore station on mainline)

[MCQ] "Pran" (PRAN-RFL Group) has major factories in Natore.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] The main economic activities in Natore include...
A) IT only
B) Agriculture (Sugar, Fruits), Agro-industry, and Trade
C) Ship building
D) Mining
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agriculture, Agro-industry, and Trade

[TRUE/FALSE] Natore Sugar Mills is a historic industry.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
