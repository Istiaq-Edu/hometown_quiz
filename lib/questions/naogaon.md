=== NAOGAON - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Naogaon is famous for which UNESCO World Heritage Site?
A) Sixty Dome Mosque
B) Somapura Mahavihara (Paharpur Buddhist Vihara)
C) Lalbagh Fort
D) Ahsan Manzil
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Somapura Mahavihara (Paharpur)

[TRUE/FALSE] Paharpur is one of the most important archaeological sites in Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Naogaon is located in which administrative division?
A) Rangpur
B) Rajshahi
C) Dhaka
D) Khulna
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rajshahi

[MCQ] "Kusumba Mosque" is a historic stone mosque in Naogaon.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] The Kusumba Mosque is depicted on the 5 Taka note.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Patisar" is associated with...
A) Kazi Nazrul Islam
B) Rabindranath Tagore (his estate/Kachari Bari)
C) Jibanananda Das
D) Lalon Shah
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rabindranath Tagore

[MCQ] "Dubalhati Rajbari" is a historic palace in Naogaon.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] Naogaon is often called the "Breadbasket" or "Rice Bowl" of Bengal (historically).
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] Which river flows through Naogaon?
A) Atrai
B) Padma
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Atrai

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] Naogaon is famous for the cultivation of...
A) Tea
B) Rice (miniket, nazirshail) and Mango (in Sapahar/Porsha)
C) Coffee
D) Rubber
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rice and Mango

[TRUE/FALSE] Sapahar and Porsha upazilas are now famous for commercial mango cultivation.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Al Kap" and "Gambhira" are folk forms popular in the region.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] Indigenous communities like Santal and Oraon have a strong presence here.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] "Panda" sweets of Naogaon are famous.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Everyday Bangladesh

[MCQ] The distance from Dhaka to Naogaon is approximately...
A) 250 km
B) 300 km
C) 400 km
D) 500 km
CORRECT ANSWER: A) 250 km

[MCQ] Which historic train station connects to Naogaon (via Santahar)?
A) Santahar Junction
B) Ishwardi
C) Bhairab
D) Akhaura
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Santahar Junction

[TRUE/FALSE] Naogaon is a major hub for rice trading.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The main economic activities in Naogaon include...
A) IT only
B) Agriculture (Rice, Mango) and Rice Mills
C) Ship building
D) Deep sea fishing
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agriculture (Rice, Mango) and Rice Mills
