=== NOAKHALI - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Noakhali was historically known as...
A) Islamabad
B) Bhulua
C) Jahangirnagar
D) Nasirabad
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bhulua

[TRUE/FALSE] Noakhali is a coastal district in the Chittagong Division.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Nijhum Dwip" (Silent Island) is located in...
A) Cox's Bazar
B) Noakhali (Hatiya Upazila)
C) Bhola
D) Sandwip
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Noakhali

[MCQ] Nijhum Dwip is famous for which animal?
A) Tigers
B) Spotted Deer (Chital)
C) Elephants
D) Lions
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Spotted Deer (Chital)

[TRUE/FALSE] Noakhali's main town is Maijdee.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Gandhi Ashram" is located in which upazila of Noakhali?
A) Begumganj
B) Sonaimuri
C) Chatkhil
D) Hatiya
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sonaimuri

[MCQ] Mahatma Gandhi visited Noakhali in...
A) 1920
B) 1946 (during communal riots)
C) 1952
D) 1971
CORRECT ANSWER: B) 1946

[TRUE/FALSE] Noakhali is known for its "Char" lands (river islands) like Char Alexander (actually in Lakshmipur but geographically related).
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Hatiya" is an...
A) Island upazila of Noakhali
B) Independent district
C) Mountain
D) Lake
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Island upazila

[MCQ] Which river flows near Noakhali?
A) Jamuna
B) Meghna
C) Padma
D) Teesta
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Meghna

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] Noakhali is famous for which traditional sweet/snack?
A) Rosogolla
B) Narikel Naru (Coconut sweets) and Muri (Puffed Rice)
C) Chomchom
D) Mishti Doi
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Narikel Naru and Muri

[TRUE/FALSE] The Noakhali dialect is distinct and widely recognized in Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Buffalo Curd" (Mohisher Doi) is popular in coastal Noakhali.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] The people of Noakhali are often jokingly referred to as being from the "Kingdom of..."
A) Royal Bengal Tiger
B) "Royal Empire"
C) "Noakhali Division" (aspirational)
D) "Mars"
CORRECT ANSWER: C) "Noakhali Division"

[TRUE/FALSE] Coconut and Betel nut (Supari) trees are abundant in Noakhali.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The main occupation in Hatiya allows for...
A) Deep sea fishing
B) Tea cultivation
C) Mining
D) IT
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Deep sea fishing

Category: Everyday Bangladesh

[MCQ] The distance from Dhaka to Noakhali is approximately...
A) 100 km
B) 160 km
C) 250 km
D) 350 km
CORRECT ANSWER: B) 160 km

[MCQ] Noakhali Science and Technology University (NSTU) is located in...
A) Hatiya
B) Sonapur/Maijdee
C) Chowmuhani
D) Feni
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sonapur/Maijdee

[TRUE/FALSE] Noakhali has direct train connectivity to Dhaka (e.g., Upakul Express).
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Chowmuhani" is a major...
A) Commercial and trade center
B) Tourist resort
C) Fishing village
D) Forest
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Commercial and trade center

[MCQ] The main economic activities in Noakhali include...
A) IT only
B) Agriculture, fishing, and remittance from expatriates
C) Heavy industry
D) Mining
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agriculture, fishing, and remittance

[TRUE/FALSE] A large number of people from Noakhali work abroad.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
