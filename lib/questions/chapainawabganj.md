=== CHAPAINAWABGANJ - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Chapainawabganj is famous as the "Capital of..."
A) Rice
B) Mango
C) Jute
D) Tea
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Mango

[TRUE/FALSE] It produces the highest amount of quality mangoes in Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Chapainawabganj is located in which administrative division?
A) Rangpur
B) Rajshahi
C) Dhaka
D) Khulna
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rajshahi

[MCQ] "Choto Sona Mosque" (Small Golden Mosque) is a historic site in...
A) Dhaka
B) Chapainawabganj (Gaud/Gaur region)
C) Bagerhat
D) Sylhet
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Chapainawabganj

[TRUE/FALSE] The Choto Sona Mosque was built during the Sultanate period.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Chapainawabganj shares a border with...
A) Myanmar
B) India (Malda/Murshidabad)
C) Nepal
D) Bhutan
CORRECT ANSWER: B) India

[MCQ] "Sonamasjid" Land Port is a major land port here.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] "Rohanpur" is known for...
A) Railway transit point with India
B) Airport
C) Sea port
D) Deep forest
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Railway transit point with India

[MCQ] Which river enters Bangladesh through Chapainawabganj?
A) Padma (Ganges)
B) Brahmaputra
C) Meghna
D) Teesta
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Padma (Ganges)

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Mahananda" river also flows through this district.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] "Gambhira" is a famous folk song/dance form originating from...
A) Sylhet
B) Chapainawabganj/Rajshahi
C) Chittagong
D) Barisal
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Chapainawabganj/Rajshahi

[TRUE/FALSE] Gambhira involves a dialogue between a maternal grandfather (Nana) and grandson (Nati).
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Kansat" is famous for...
A) Mango market
B) Fish market
C) IT park
D) Tea auction
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Mango market

[MCQ] Chapainawabganj is famous for "Kalai Ruti" (Bread made of Mashkalai dal).
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Nakshi Kantha (Embroidered Quilt) making is a tradition.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Everyday Bangladesh

[MCQ] The distance from Dhaka to Chapainawabganj is approximately...
A) 250 km
B) 320 km
C) 400 km
D) 500 km
CORRECT ANSWER: B) 320 km

[MCQ] "Banalata Express" is a non-stop train connecting Dhaka to...
A) Khulna
B) Chapainawabganj (via Rajshahi)
C) Sylhet
D) Rangpur
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Chapainawabganj

[TRUE/FALSE] The mango economy is seasonal but massive.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The main economic activities in Chapainawabganj include...
A) IT only
B) Mango cultivation, Agriculture, and Land Port trade
C) Ship building
D) Deep sea fishing
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Mango cultivation, Agriculture, and Land Port trade

[TRUE/FALSE] Silk production (Sericulture) is also present.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
