=== MYMENSINGH - RE-CATEGORIZED AND EXPANDED QUIZ ===
Category: Places and History (Minimum 10 Questions)
This category focuses on specific landmarks, geographical features, historical events, and significant figures associated with Mymensingh's past and present.

[MCQ] Mymensingh is situated on the banks of which river?
A) Old Brahmaputra
B) Jamuna
C) Meghna
D) Dhaleshwari
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Old Brahmaputra
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Shashi Lodge" is a famous historical palace in Mymensingh city.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Mymensingh was historically known as...
A) Nasirabad
B) Jahangirnagar
C) Islamabad
D) Khalifatabad
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Nasirabad
[MCQ] The "Bangladesh Agricultural University" (BAU), the premier agricultural university, is located in...
A) Gazipur
B) Mymensingh
C) Dhaka
D) Comilla
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Mymensingh
[MCQ] "Zainul Abedin Sangrahashala" (Art Gallery) in Mymensingh is dedicated to which famous artist?
A) SM Sultan
B) Zainul Abedin
C) Quamrul Hassan
D) Hashem Khan
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Zainul Abedin
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Muktagacha" Zamindar Bari is famous for its ancient architecture.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which famous sweet is Muktagacha (a sub-district of Mymensingh) known for?
A) Rosogolla
B) Monda
C) Chomchom
D) Sandesh
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Monda
[MCQ] "Alexander Castle" or "Loha Kutir" is a historic building in Mymensingh visited by...
A) Rabindranath Tagore
B) Mahatma Gandhi
C) Lord Curzon
D) All of the above
CORRECT ANSWER: D) All of the above (Many dignitaries visited it).
[TRUE/FALSE] Mymensingh became the 8th administrative division of Bangladesh in 2015.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] The "Garo" indigenous community primarily lives in the border areas of Mymensingh and...
A) Sylhet
B) Tangail/Sherpur/Netrokona
C) Chittagong
D) Rajshahi
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Tangail/Sherpur/Netrokona (Haluaghat/Madhupur areas).
[MCQ] "Bipin Park" is a historic park located in Mymensingh city near the...
A) Railway Station
B) Brahmaputra River
C) Shashi Lodge
D) Circuit House
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Brahmaputra River
[TRUE/FALSE] Ananda Mohan College in Mymensingh is one of the oldest colleges in the country.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Gouripur Lodge" is another historical building located in...
A) Mymensingh City
B) Gouripur Upazila
C) Muktagacha
D) Trishal
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Mymensingh City (Built by Zamindars of Gouripur).
[MCQ] The "Silver Castle" is a popular resort located in Mymensingh.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True
[MCQ] "Birishiri" in Durgapur (Netrokona, Mymensingh Division) is famous for...
A) Ceramic hills (China Matir Pahar) and Shomeswari River
B) Tea gardens
C) Sea beach
D) Mangrove forest
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Ceramic hills (China Matir Pahar) and Shomeswari River
[MCQ] The "Madhupur Tract" (Madhupur Garh) is a forest area located partly in Mymensingh and Tangail.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True
[MCQ] "Sherpur" district in Mymensingh division is known for the "Ghazni" tourist spot.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True
[MCQ] Who was the legendary leader of the "Tebhaga Movement" from this region (specifically Nachol, but influenced the north)?
A) Ila Mitra
B) Pritilata
C) Begum Rokeya
D) Sufia Kamal
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Ila Mitra (Though Nachol is in Rajshahi, the movement had ripples. But Moni Singh of Netrokona is the key communist leader here). *Correction: Moni Singh is the key figure for Mymensingh/Netrokona Tonk movement.*
[MCQ] "Comrade Moni Singh" is associated with the...
A) Tonk Movement
B) Language Movement
C) Fakir Rebellion
D) Sepoy Mutiny
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Tonk Movement


Category: Culture and Traditions (Minimum 10 Questions)
This category covers festivals, arts, crafts, specific cultural practices, and aspects that define the unique identity and heritage of Mymensingh and its people.

[MCQ] "Mymensingh Gitika" (Ballads of Mymensingh) is a famous collection of...
A) Modern poems
B) Folk ballads/songs
C) Short stories
D) Religious texts
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Folk ballads/songs
[TRUE/FALSE] "Mahua" and "Malua" are famous characters from the Mymensingh Gitika.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] The "Garo" community celebrates a harvest festival called...
A) Wangala
B) Baisabi
C) Karam
D) Sohrai
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Wangala
[MCQ] "Jari Gaan" is a popular folk music genre in Mymensingh, often involving...
A) Solo singing
B) Debate/Battle between two groups
C) Instrumental only
D) Silent performance
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Debate/Battle between two groups (Jari means debate).
[TRUE/FALSE] "Nakshi Kantha" (embroidered quilt) has a strong tradition in Mymensingh (e.g., Taposhi Rabeya's works).
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which famous writer and humorist was from Mymensingh (Netrokona)?
A) Humayun Ahmed
B) Zafar Iqbal
C) Nirmalendu Goon
D) All of the above
CORRECT ANSWER: D) All of the above (They are from the greater Mymensingh/Netrokona region).
[MCQ] "Baul" songs are very popular in the Mymensingh region. Who is a famous Baul from this area (Netrokona)?
A) Lalon Shah
B) Shah Abdul Karim
C) Ukil Munshi
D) Hason Raja
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Ukil Munshi (and also Jalal Uddin Khan).
[TRUE/FALSE] "Monda" of Muktagacha is made primarily from milk and sugar/jaggery.

[MCQ] The "Jatiya Kabi Kazi Nazrul Islam University" is located in...
A) Trishal, Mymensingh

C) Comilla
D) Churulia
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Trishal, Mymensingh (Where Nazrul stayed in his childhood).
[MCQ] "Ha-Du-Du" (Kabaddi) is a popular traditional sport in rural Mymensingh.

[MCQ] The "Botanical Garden" at Bangladesh Agricultural University is famous for its...
