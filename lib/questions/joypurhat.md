=== JOYPURHAT - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Joypurhat was formerly part of which district?
A) Bogra
B) Rajshahi
C) Rangpur
D) Pabna
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Bogra

[TRUE/FALSE] Joypurhat is known as the "Smallest District" of Rajshahi Division (in population/area context, often cited).
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Joypurhat is located in which administrative division?
A) Rangpur
B) Rajshahi
C) Dhaka
D) Khulna
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rajshahi

[MCQ] "Paharpur Buddhist Vihara" is located nearby in Naogaon, but Joypurhat is the...
A) Nearest railway station (Jamalganj/Joypurhat) for travelers
B) Air base
C) Sea port
D) Capital
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Nearest railway station

[MCQ] "Hinda - Kashba Shahi Mosque" is a historic mosque in...
A) Joypurhat
B) Bogra
C) Naogaon
D) Sirajganj
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Joypurhat

[TRUE/FALSE] Joypurhat has limestone mines.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Jamalganj Coal Mine" is located in...
A) Dinajpur
B) Joypurhat
C) Rangpur
D) Sylhet
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Joypurhat

[MCQ] Joypurhat Sugar Mills is a significant industry.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] Which river flows through Joypurhat?
A) Little Jamuna and Tulshiganga
B) Padma
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Little Jamuna and Tulshiganga

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] Joypurhat has a rich tradition of folk music including...
A) Bhawaiya
B) Jari Gan
C) Gambhira
D) Baul
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Bhawaiya (influenced by North Bengal) and Palligeeti

[TRUE/FALSE] Poultry farming is a massive industry in Joypurhat ("Poultry Capital").
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Joypurhat is heavily involved in the production of...
A) Potato and Poultry
B) Tea
C) Rubber
D) Hilsa fish
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Potato and Poultry

[MCQ] "Baro Shibalaya" (Twelve Shiva Temples) is a historic Hindu site.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Indigenous communities like Santal have a presence here.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Everyday Bangladesh

[MCQ] The distance from Dhaka to Joypurhat is approximately...
A) 250 km
B) 280 km
C) 400 km
D) 500 km
CORRECT ANSWER: B) 280 km (via Bogra)

[MCQ] Joypurhat is well connected by train to Dhaka.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] "Ekota Express" and "Drutojan Express" stop at Joypurhat.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] The main economic activities in Joypurhat include...
A) IT only
B) Poultry, Agriculture (Potato), and Sugar industry
C) Ship building
D) Mining (active Gold)
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Poultry, Agriculture, and Sugar industry

[TRUE/FALSE] Joypurhat supplies a large portion of chicken and eggs to the country.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
