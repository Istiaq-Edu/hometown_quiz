=== SUNAMGANJ - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Sunamganj is famous for which natural feature?
A) Mountains
B) Haors (large wetlands)
C) Deserts
D) Beaches
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Haors (large wetlands)

[TRUE/FALSE] "Tanguar Haor" is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
CORRECT ANSWER: False (It's a Ramsar site, not UNESCO World Heritage)

[MCQ] "Tanguar Haor" is designated as a...
A) National Park
B) Ramsar Wetland of International Importance
C) Wildlife Sanctuary only
D) Reserved Forest
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Ramsar Wetland of International Importance

[MCQ] Sunamganj is located in which administrative division?
A) Dhaka
B) Sylhet
C) Chittagong
D) Mymensingh
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sylhet

[TRUE/FALSE] Sunamganj has significant haor areas covering a large portion of the district.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river flows through Sunamganj?
A) Padma
B) Surma River
C) Meghna
D) Jamuna
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Surma River

[MCQ] The "Hasong Raja" museum is dedicated to which famous poet?
A) Rabindranath Tagore
B) Hason Raja
C) Kazi Nazrul Islam
D) Lalon Shah
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Hason Raja

[TRUE/FALSE] Hason Raja was a famous mystic poet from Sunamganj.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Shimul Bagan" (Shimul garden) with red cotton trees is located in...
A) Sunamganj Sadar
B) Tahirpur
C) Jamalganj
D) Dharampasha
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Tahirpur

[MCQ] The "Barek Tila" is a...
A) River
B) Hill/elevated land in haor area
C) Temple
D) Mosque
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Hill/elevated land in haor area

[TRUE/FALSE] Sunamganj is known for its scenic beauty during monsoon when haors are flooded.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Tekerghat" is famous for its...
A) Beach
B) Limestone quarries and scenic hills
C) Desert
D) Tea gardens
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Limestone quarries and scenic hills

[MCQ] The "Jadukata River" is known for its...
A) Pollution
B) Crystal clear water
C) Bridges
D) Industrial use
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Crystal clear water

[TRUE/FALSE] Sunamganj shares a border with India (Meghalaya).
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Niladri Lake" (also known as Shankarpur Lake) is located in...
A) Sunamganj Sadar
B) Tahirpur
C) Bishwamvarpur
D) Derai
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Tahirpur

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] Sunamganj is known for which folk music tradition?
A) Classical only
B) Baul and mystic songs (especially Hason Raja songs)
C) Rock music
D) Western classical
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Baul and mystic songs

[TRUE/FALSE] Hason Raja's songs are still popular in Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Sylheti" dialect is spoken in Sunamganj.

B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] Which fish is abundant in Sunamganj's haors?
A) Marine fish
B) Various freshwater fish (Boal, Ayre, Rui, etc.)

CORRECT ANSWER: B) Various freshwater fish

[TRUE/FALSE] Fishing is a major occupation in Sunamganj.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Pitha" and sweets in Sunamganj are popular during...
A) Summer
B) Winter and festivals
C) Monsoon only
D) Autumn only
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Winter and festivals

[MCQ] Traditional boat racing is popular in Sunamganj's...
A) Mountains
B) Haors and rivers
C) Deserts
D) Forests only
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Haors and rivers

[TRUE/FALSE] The haor communities have distinct lifestyles adapted to wetland living.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Rice cultivation in haor areas is primarily...
A) Year-round
B) Boro rice (dry season)
C) Only monsoon
D) Not practiced
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Boro rice (dry season)

[MCQ] Duck rearing is common in haor areas.

CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Stone collection from rivers is an economic activity near the Indian border.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
