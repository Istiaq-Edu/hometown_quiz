=== NARSINGDI - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Narsingdi is famous for which archaeological site?
A) Mahasthangarh
B) Wari-Bateshwar
C) Paharpur
D) Mainamati
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Wari-Bateshwar

[TRUE/FALSE] Wari-Bateshwar is an ancient fort city dating back to 450 BC.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Narsingdi is located in which administrative division?
A) Chittagong
B) Dhaka
C) Sylhet
D) Mymensingh
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Dhaka

[MCQ] Which river flows through Narsingdi?
A) Jamuna
B) Meghna, Shitalakshya, and Arial Khan
C) Karnaphuli
D) Teesta
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Meghna, Shitalakshya, and Arial Khan

[MCQ] Narsingdi is known as the...
A) City of Silk
B) Manchester of the East (for textile industry)
C) City of Mosques
D) Tea Capital
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Manchester of the East

[TRUE/FALSE] Narsingdi has a very strong textile industry, especially Baburhat (Shekherchar).
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Baburhat" is famous for...
A) Cattle market
B) Wholesale cloth market (Kaporer Haat)
C) Fish market
D) Vegetable market
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Wholesale cloth market

[MCQ] "Sonaimuri Tek" is a...
A) Historical hillock and picnic spot
B) River port
C) Deep forest
D) Tea garden
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Historical hillock and picnic spot

[TRUE/FALSE] Narsingdi produces high-quality bananas (Sagar Kola).
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Dream Holiday Park" is a popular theme park located in...
A) Gazipur
B) Narsingdi
C) Dhaka
D) Comilla
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Narsingdi

[MCQ] "Ghorashal" is famous for...
A) Tea gardens
B) Power Station and Fertilizer Factory
C) Jute mills only
D) Historic temples
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Power Station and Fertilizer Factory

[TRUE/FALSE] Narsingdi is well-connected to Dhaka by rail and road.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] Narsingdi is famous for which fruit?
A) Mango
B) Banana (Amrit Sagar) and Litchi
C) Jackfruit
D) Pineapple
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Banana (Amrit Sagar) and Litchi

[TRUE/FALSE] The textile industry is the main economic driver of Narsingdi.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Lungi" and "Gamcha" production is significant in...
A) Sylhet
B) Narsingdi
C) Barisal
D) Khulna
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Narsingdi

[MCQ] The dialect of Narsingdi is close to...
A) Chittagonian
B) Standard Bengali with Dhaka regional influence
C) Sylheti
D) Rangpuri
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Standard Bengali with Dhaka regional influence

[TRUE/FALSE] "Pitha" culture is strong in rural Narsingdi during winter.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Traditional weaving (Tat) is a major profession in...
A) Mountains
B) Narsingdi's weaving villages
C) Coastal areas
D) Tea gardens
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Narsingdi's weaving villages

Category: Everyday Bangladesh

[MCQ] The distance from Dhaka to Narsingdi is approximately...
A) 20 km
B) 50 km
C) 100 km
D) 150 km
CORRECT ANSWER: B) 50 km

[MCQ] Narsingdi has a major railway junction at...
A) Bhairab
B) Narsingdi Sadar
C) Tongi
D) Akhaura
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Narsingdi Sadar

[TRUE/FALSE] The Dhaka-Sylhet highway passes through Narsingdi.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The main economic activities in Narsingdi include...
A) IT only
B) Textile industry, agriculture, and trade
C) Mining
D) Tourism only
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Textile industry, agriculture, and trade

[MCQ] Which historic rebel leader against British rule is associated with Narsingdi (Raipura)?
A) Titumir
B) Bir Shreshtha Matiur Rahman (born here)
C) Surya Sen
D) Pritilata
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bir Shreshtha Matiur Rahman

[TRUE/FALSE] Narsingdi is one of the most developed districts in terms of industry.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
