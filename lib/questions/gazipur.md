=== GAZIPUR - RE-CATEGORIZED AND EXPANDED QUIZ ===

Category: Places & History (Minimum 15 Questions)
Historical sites, landmarks, and the rich heritage of Gazipur.

[MCQ] What is the most famous landmark in Gazipur, symbolizing the Liberation War of 1971?
A) Shaheed Minar
B) Jagroto Chowrangi (The Awakened)
C) National Martyrs' Memorial (Jatiyo Smriti Soudho)
D) Bangabandhu Memorial
CORRECT ANSWER: C) National Martyrs' Memorial (Jatiyo Smriti Soudho)

[TRUE/FALSE] The National Martyrs' Memorial (Jatiyo Smriti Soudho) in Savar is located within Gazipur district.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which major university campus is located in Gazipur district?
A) Dhaka University
B) Jahangirnagar University
C) BUET
D) Jagannath University
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Jahangirnagar University

[MCQ] The "Bhawal Rajbari" (Bhawal King's Palace) is a historic landmark located in...
A) Savar
B) Tongi
C) Joydebpur (Gazipur Sadar)
D) Kaliakair
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Joydebpur (Gazipur Sadar)

[TRUE/FALSE] The Bhawal National Park is one of the oldest national parks in Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The famous "Bhawal Sannyas Case" was a legal case about...
A) Land dispute
B) A prince claiming his identity after being presumed dead
C) Religious property
D) Royal treasure
CORRECT ANSWER: B) A prince claiming his identity after being presumed dead

[MCQ] Which river flows through Gazipur district?
A) Padma
B) Turag
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Turag

[TRUE/FALSE] Gazipur was named after Ghazi Shahib, a Sufi saint.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Pubail" area in Gazipur is famous for...
A) Industrial zone
B) Historic mosque and archaeological site
C) River port
D) Tea gardens
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Historic mosque and archaeological site

[MCQ] Which upazila of Gazipur is famous for the "Sadullahpur Mosque"?
A) Savar
B) Kaliganj
C) Kapasia
D) Sreepur
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Kaliganj

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Rajendrapur National Park" is located in Gazipur.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Bhawal Badre Alam Government College" is located in...
A) Tongi
B) Joydebpur
C) Savar
D) Kaliakair
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Joydebpur

[MCQ] Which famous cantonment is located in Gazipur?
A) Comilla Cantonment
B) Savar Cantonment
C) Bogra Cantonment
D) Jessore Cantonment
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Savar Cantonment

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Tongi Ijtema" is held in Gazipur district, making it one of the largest Islamic congregations in the world.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Rani Bilasmoni Government Boy's High School" is one of the oldest schools in...
A) Dhaka
B) Gazipur
C) Tangail
D) Mymensingh
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Gazipur

[MCQ] The "Chandra" area in Gazipur is known for its...
A) Furniture market
B) Garments factories
C) Agricultural farms
D) Fish market
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Garments factories

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Bhitargarh" archaeological site is located in Gazipur.
CORRECT ANSWER: False (It's in Panchagarh)

[MCQ] Which historical figure's mazar (shrine) is located in Tongi?
A) Shah Jalal
B) Khan Jahan Ali
C) Hazrat Shah Ali Bogdadi
D) Bayazid Bostami
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Hazrat Shah Ali Bogdadi

Category: Culture & Traditions (Minimum 15 Questions)
Local customs, festivals, food, and the cultural identity of Gazipur.

[MCQ] Which annual religious congregation in Tongi, Gazipur, attracts millions of Muslims from around the world?
A) Eid Jamaat
B) Biswa Ijtema (World Congregation)
C) Tabligh Conference
D) Islamic Summit
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Biswa Ijtema (World Congregation)

[TRUE/FALSE] The Biswa Ijtema is held on the banks of the Turag River.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Gazipur is famous for which type of industry?
A) Jute
B) Ready-Made Garments (RMG)
C) Shipbuilding
D) Tea processing
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Ready-Made Garments (RMG)

[MCQ] The "Bhawal Mela" is a traditional fair held in...
A) Tongi
B) Joydebpur
C) Savar
D) Kaliakair
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Joydebpur

[TRUE/FALSE] Gazipur is known for its pineapple cultivation.
CORRECT ANSWER: True (Particularly in Sreepur area)

[MCQ] Which sweet is traditionally associated with the Gazipur/Bhawal region?
A) Rosogolla
B) Chomchom
C) Sandesh
D) Mishti Doi
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Sandesh

[MCQ] The "Pahela Baishakh" celebrations in Gazipur often feature...
A) Boat races
B) Traditional fairs and cultural programs
C) Horse racing
D) Bull fighting
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Traditional fairs and cultural programs

[TRUE/FALSE] Gazipur has a significant population of garment workers, making it a major hub for labor-intensive industries.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Kaliakair" upazila is known for...
A) Hi-tech parks and industrial zones
B) Tea gardens
C) Fishing
D) Silk production
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Hi-tech parks and industrial zones

[MCQ] Which craft is traditionally practiced in some rural areas of Gazipur?
A) Nakshi Kantha
B) Jamdani weaving
C) Pottery
D) Shital Pati
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Pottery

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujib Hi-Tech Park" is located in Gazipur.
CORRECT ANSWER: True (Specifically in Kaliakair)

[MCQ] The "Savar Dairy Farm" is famous for producing...
A) Beef
B) Milk and dairy products
C) Poultry
D) Honey
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Milk and dairy products

[MCQ] Which flower is cultivated in the nurseries of Gazipur, supplying much of Dhaka's demand?
A) Roses
B) Orchids
C) Various flowers (It's a major flower hub)
D) Lilies only
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Various flowers (It's a major flower hub)

[TRUE/FALSE] Gazipur has a strong tradition of "Jari Gaan" folk music.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Kapasia" upazila is known for its...
A) Haor (wetlands)
B) Hills
C) Mangrove forest
D) Desert
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Haor (wetlands)
