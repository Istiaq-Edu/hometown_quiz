=== NARAIL - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Narail is located in which administrative division?
A) Rajshahi
B) Khulna
C) Dhaka
D) Barisal
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Khulna

[TRUE/FALSE] Narail was part of greater Jessore district.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river flows through Narail?
A) Padma
B) Nabaganga and Chitra
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Nabaganga and Chitra

[MCQ] Narail is the birthplace of which famous artist?
A) Zainul Abedin
B) S.M. Sultan
C) Quamrul Hasan
D) Rashid Choudhury
CORRECT ANSWER: B) S.M. Sultan

[TRUE/FALSE] S.M. Sultan is a renowned Bangladeshi painter known for his rural scenes.

[MCQ] The "S.M. Sultan Memorial" is located in Narail.

B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] "Lohagara" upazila is known for...

B) Agricultural production

D) Beach
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural production

[TRUE/FALSE] Narail has flat terrain suitable for agriculture.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Kalia" upazila is known for...
A) Hills

CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural areas

[MCQ] Narail has historic zamindari architecture.
