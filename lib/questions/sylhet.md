=== SYLHET - RE-CATEGORIZED AND EXPANDED QUIZ ===
Category: Places and History (Minimum 10 Questions)
This category focuses on specific landmarks, geographical features, historical events, and significant figures associated with Sylhet's past and present.

[MCQ] Sylhet is famously known as the "Land of..."?
A) Rivers
B) Two Leaves and a Bud
C) Mosques
D) Hills
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Two Leaves and a Bud (Referring to tea gardens).
[TRUE/FALSE] The shrine of Hazrat Shah Jalal (R) is located in Sylhet.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which river flows through the city of Sylhet?
A) Surma
B) Kushiara
C) Meghna
D) Jamuna
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Surma
[MCQ] "Jaflong" is a famous tourist spot in Sylhet known for its...
A) Sea beach
B) Stone collection and rolling hills
C) Mangrove forest
D) Coral reef
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Stone collection and rolling hills
[MCQ] The "Keane Bridge" is a historic landmark in Sylhet city. Which river does it cross?
A) Surma
B) Kushiara
C) Piyain
D) Sari
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Surma
[TRUE/FALSE] Sylhet was once part of the Assam province during British rule.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which famous swamp forest is located in Sylhet (Gowainghat)?
A) Sundarbans
B) Ratargul
C) Lawachara
D) Bhawal
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Ratargul
[MCQ] The "Ali Amjad's Clock" is a famous historical clock tower located near...
A) Shah Jalal University
B) Keane Bridge
C) Osmani Airport
D) Zindabazar
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Keane Bridge
[TRUE/FALSE] Sri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu, the founder of Gaudiya Vaishnavism, has his ancestral home in Sylhet.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which tea garden in Sylhet is often cited as the first commercial tea garden in Bangladesh (established in 1854)?
A) Malnicherra Tea Estate
B) Lakkatura Tea Estate
C) Finlay Tea Estate
D) Kazi & Kazi Tea Estate
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Malnicherra Tea Estate
[MCQ] "Bichnakandi" is a scenic spot in Sylhet located on the border with...
A) Myanmar
B) India (Meghalaya)
C) Tripura
D) West Bengal
CORRECT ANSWER: B) India (Meghalaya)
[TRUE/FALSE] General M.A.G. Osmani, the Commander-in-Chief of the Bangladesh Forces during the Liberation War, was from Sylhet.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] The "Lalakhal" is a canal in Sylhet famous for the color of its water. What color is it?
A) Red
B) Emerald Blue/Green
C) Black
D) Yellow
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Emerald Blue/Green
[MCQ] "Pangthumai" waterfall is located in which Upazila of Sylhet?
A) Gowainghat
B) Kanaighat
C) Jaintiapur
D) Beanibazar
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Gowainghat
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Jaintia Rajbari" (Kingdom of Jaintia) ruins are located in Sylhet.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which famous earthquake in 1897 caused massive destruction in Sylhet?
A) The Great Indian Earthquake (Assam Earthquake)
B) Nepal Earthquake
C) Bhuj Earthquake
D) Kashmir Earthquake
CORRECT ANSWER: A) The Great Indian Earthquake (Assam Earthquake)
[MCQ] "Madhabkunda Waterfall", the largest waterfall in Bangladesh, is located in which district of Sylhet division?
A) Sylhet
B) Moulvibazar
C) Habiganj
D) Sunamganj
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Moulvibazar
[TRUE/FALSE] "Tanguar Haor", a Ramsar site, is located in the Sunamganj district of Sylhet division.
CORRECT ANSWER: True


Category: Culture and Traditions (Minimum 10 Questions)
This category covers festivals, arts, crafts, specific cultural practices, and aspects that define the unique identity and heritage of Sylhet and its people.

[MCQ] The "Sylheti" language/dialect is written in which script (historically)?
A) Bengali
B) Sylheti Nagri
C) Arabic
D) Devanagari
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sylheti Nagri
[TRUE/FALSE] "Satkora" (Citrus macroptera) is a citrus fruit widely used in Sylheti cuisine, especially with beef.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which traditional dance is associated with the Manipuri community in Sylhet?
A) Jhumur
B) Manipuri Dance
C) Chhau
D) Gambhira
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Manipuri Dance
[MCQ] "Hason Raja" was a famous mystic poet and songwriter from Sylhet. What was his primary theme?
A) Revolution
B) Nature
C) Spiritualism and impermanence
D) Romance
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Spiritualism and impermanence
[TRUE/FALSE] "Sital Pati" (Cool Mat) is a traditional craft of Sylhet recognized by UNESCO.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which festival is celebrated with great enthusiasm by the tea garden workers in Sylhet?
A) Fagua
B) Rash Mela
C) Jhulan Yatra
D) Shakrain
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Fagua
[MCQ] "Nunor Bora" is a popular savory snack in Sylhet made from...
A) Rice flour
B) Wheat flour
C) Lentils
D) Potato
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Rice flour
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Urs" of Shah Jalal is a major annual religious gathering in Sylhet.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Dhamail" is a folk dance and song form performed primarily by...
A) Men during harvest
B) Women during weddings and festivals
C) Children during school events
D) Fishermen
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Women during weddings and festivals
[MCQ] Shah Abdul Karim, the legendary Baul emperor, hailed from which district in the Sylhet division?
A) Sylhet
B) Sunamganj
C) Habiganj
D) Moulvibazar
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sunamganj
[TRUE/FALSE] Sylhet is known for its distinct "Seven Layers Tea".
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Tenga" (Sour curry) is a staple in Sylheti cuisine. It is often made with...
A) Mango
B) Tamarind or Elephant Apple (Chalta) or Lemon
C) Vinegar
D) Yogurt
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Tamarind or Elephant Apple (Chalta) or Lemon
[MCQ] The "Khasia" community in Sylhet is known for cultivating...
A) Rice
B) Betel Leaf (Pan)
C) Wheat
D) Jute
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Betel Leaf (Pan)
[MCQ] "Chunga Pitha" is a traditional rice cake of Sylhet cooked inside...
A) Banana leaf
B) Bamboo tube
C) Clay pot
D) Iron pan
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bamboo tube
[TRUE/FALSE] "Binni Dhan" (Sticky rice) is commonly used in Sylheti cuisine.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Radharaman Dutta" was a famous composer of...
A) Dhamail songs
B) Baul songs
C) Modern songs
D) Classical music
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Dhamail songs


Category: Everyday Bangladesh (Sylhet Focus) (Minimum 10 Questions)

[MCQ] What is the name of the international airport in Sylhet?
A) Shah Amanat International Airport
B) Osmani International Airport
C) Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport
D) Saidpur Airport
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Osmani International Airport
[MCQ] Sylhet is a major hub for remittances due to a large diaspora population living in...
A) USA
B) UK (United Kingdom)

D) Canada

A) Sylhet Agricultural University
B) Shahjalal University of Science and Technology (SUST)

D) Leading University

[TRUE/FALSE] Natural gas is a major natural resource found in the Sylhet region.

[MCQ] Which major highway connects Sylhet to Dhaka?
A) N1
B) N2

[TRUE/FALSE] Sylhet has a cooler climate and higher rainfall compared to many other parts of Bangladesh.

[MCQ] The "MAG Osmani Medical College" is located in...
A) Ambarkhana
B) Zindabazar

[MCQ] "Zindabazar" is the...
A) Main commercial center/shopping hub of Sylhet city
B) Residential area
C) Industrial area

[MCQ] Which bridge over the Surma river is known as the "New Bridge" (Shah Jalal Bridge)?
