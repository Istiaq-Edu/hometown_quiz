=== KHAGRACHARI - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Khagrachari is located in which region?
A) Barind Tract
B) Chittagong Hill Tracts (CHT)
C) Sylhet Division
D) Sundarbans
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Chittagong Hill Tracts (CHT)

[TRUE/FALSE] Khagrachari is one of the three hill districts in Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Alutila Cave" is a famous tourist attraction in...
A) Rangamati
B) Khagrachari
C) Bandarban
D) Chittagong
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Khagrachari

[MCQ] The "Alutila Mysterious Cave" requires visitors to...
A) Swim through
B) Walk through with torches (dark cave with stream)
C) Climb mountains
D) Take a boat
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Walk through with torches

[TRUE/FALSE] The Alutila hill offers a panoramic view of Khagrachari town.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Richhang Waterfall" is located in...
A) Khagrachari Sadar
B) Near Khagrachari town
C) Bandarban
D) Rangamati
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Near Khagrachari town

[MCQ] Which indigenous communities live in Khagrachari?
A) Only Chakma
B) Chakma, Marma, Tripura, and others
C) Bengali only
D) Garo only
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Chakma, Marma, Tripura, and others

[TRUE/FALSE] The Tripura community has a significant presence in Khagrachari.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Chengi River" flows through Khagrachari.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] "Dighinala" upazila is known for...
A) Beach
B) Historic sites and indigenous villages
C) Industrial zone
D) Airport
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Historic sites and indigenous villages

[TRUE/FALSE] Khagrachari has hilly terrain with forests.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Tirthapuri Hindu Temple" is significant for...
A) Muslims
B) Hindu pilgrims
C) Buddhists only
D) Christians only
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Hindu pilgrims

[MCQ] "Mahalchari" upazila is known for...
A) Urban development
B) Rivers and indigenous communities
C) Industrial zone
D) Airport
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rivers and indigenous communities

[TRUE/FALSE] Khagrachari's landscape includes hills, valleys, and rivers.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Pablakhali Wildlife Sanctuary" is known for...
A) Marine life
B) Elephants and wildlife

CORRECT ANSWER: B) Elephants and wildlife
