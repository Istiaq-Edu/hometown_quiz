=== BAGERHAT - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Bagerhat is famous for which UNESCO World Heritage Site?
A) Lalbagh Fort
B) Historic Mosque City of Bagerhat (Sixty Dome Mosque)
C) Paharpur
D) Mahasthangarh
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Historic Mosque City of Bagerhat

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Shait Gombuj Masjid" (Sixty Dome Mosque) is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The Sixty Dome Mosque was built by...
A) Mughal Emperor
B) Khan Jahan Ali
C) British officers
D) Modern architects
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Khan Jahan Ali

[MCQ] Khan Jahan Ali was a...
A) Musician
B) Medieval Muslim saint and administrator
C) Politician
D) Cricketer
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Medieval Muslim saint and administrator

[TRUE/FALSE] Khan Jahan Ali established many mosques and infrastructure in Bagerhat.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Bagerhat is located in which administrative division?
A) Rajshahi
B) Khulna
C) Dhaka
D) Barisal
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Khulna

[MCQ] The "Sundarbans" is partially located in Bagerhat.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Bagerhat has access to the Sundarbans mangrove forest.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river flows near Bagerhat?
A) Padma
B) Rupsha, Bhairab, and Sundarbans waterways
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rupsha, Bhairab, and Sundarbans waterways

[MCQ] "Mongla" is a famous...
A) Hill station
B) Sea port
C) Desert
D) Mountain
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sea port

[TRUE/FALSE] Mongla Port is one of the major sea ports of Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Khan Jahan Ali Mazar" (tomb) is a pilgrimage site.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Bagerhat has many historic mosques from the 15th century.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Morrelganj" upazila is known for...
A) Mountains
B) Sundarbans adjacent area
C) Desert
D) Tea gardens
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sundarbans adjacent area
