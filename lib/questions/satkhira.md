=== SATKHIRA - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Satkhira is located in which administrative division?
A) Rajshahi
B) Khulna
C) Dhaka
D) Barisal
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Khulna

[TRUE/FALSE] Satkhira is adjacent to the Sundarbans.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Sundarbans" World Heritage Site is partially in Satkhira.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] Satkhira shares a border with which country?
A) Myanmar
B) India (West Bengal)
C) Nepal
D) Bhutan
CORRECT ANSWER: B) India (West Bengal)

[TRUE/FALSE] Satkhira is a border district.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river system is near Satkhira?
A) Padma
B) Kopotaksha and Ichamati (Sundarbans rivers)
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Kopotaksha and Ichamati

[MCQ] "Shyamnagar" upazila is known for...
A) Mountains
B) Sundarbans and shrimp farming
C) Desert
D) Tea gardens
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sundarbans and shrimp farming

[TRUE/FALSE] Shrimp (Bagda and Golda) farming is significant in Satkhira.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Tala" upazila is known for...
A) Hills
B) Agricultural areas
C) Tea gardens
D) Sea port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural areas

[MCQ] The "Sundarbans East Wildlife Sanctuary" is near Satkhira.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Royal Bengal Tigers live in the Sundarbans near Satkhira.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Kalaroa" upazila is known for...
A) Mountains
B) Agricultural production
C) Desert
D) Beach
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural production
