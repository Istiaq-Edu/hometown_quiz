=== PANCHAGARH - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Panchagarh is the...
A) Southernmost district
B) Northernmost district of Bangladesh
C) Easternmost district
D) Westernmost district
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Northernmost district

[TRUE/FALSE] Panchagarh is often called the "Daughter of the Himalayas".
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] From Panchagarh (Tetulia), one can see...
A) The Bay of Bengal
B) Kanchenjunga peak (Himalayas)
C) The Sundarbans
D) Cox's Bazar
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Kanchenjunga peak

[MCQ] "Tetulia" is famous for being...
A) A sea port
B) The northernmost point/upazila and tea gardens
C) An island
D) A dense forest
CORRECT ANSWER: B) The northernmost point and tea gardens

[TRUE/FALSE] Panchagarh is the only district in Bangladesh where tea is grown in plain lands.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Banglabandha" Land Port connects Bangladesh with...
A) Myanmar
B) India (and route to Nepal/Bhutan)
C) China
D) Thailand
CORRECT ANSWER: B) India (and route to Nepal/Bhutan)

[TRUE/FALSE] Banglabandha is a "Zero Point" border.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river flows through Panchagarh?
A) Kartoa
B) Padma
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Kartoa

[MCQ] "Mirzapur Shahi Mosque" is a historic mosque in Panchagarh.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] "Bhitargarh" is a...
A) Historic fortified city and archaeological site
B) Modern shopping mall
C) Sea beach
D) River port
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Historic fortified city

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] Panchagarh is distinctive for its...
A) Flat land tea cultivation
B) Deep sea fishing
C) Salt production
D) Ship breaking
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Flat land tea cultivation

[TRUE/FALSE] The culture has influences from Rajbanshi and North Bengal traditions.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Bhawaiya" songs are popular in this region.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] Rocks/Stones are a major natural resource extracted in...
A) Dhaka
B) Panchagarh (from rivers)
C) Barisal
D) Khulna
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Panchagarh

[TRUE/FALSE] Stone crushing and sand lifting are economic activities.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Everyday Bangladesh

[MCQ] The distance from Dhaka to Panchagarh is approximately...
A) 200 km
B) 300 km
C) 450 km
D) 600 km
CORRECT ANSWER: C) 450 km

[MCQ] Panchagarh has direct train connectivity to Dhaka via...
A) Ekota, Drutojan, and Panchagarh Express
B) Subarna Express
C) Parabat Express
D) Upakul Express
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Ekota, Drutojan, and Panchagarh Express

[TRUE/FALSE] It is the longest direct train route in Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Winters are generally...
A) Mild
B) Severe/Coldest in Bangladesh
C) Hot
D) Rainy
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Severe/Coldest in Bangladesh

[MCQ] The main economic activities in Panchagarh include...
A) IT only
B) Agriculture (Tea, Stones), and trade via land port
C) Ship building
D) Deep sea tourism
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agriculture (Tea, Stones), and trade via land port

[TRUE/FALSE] Kanchenjunga viewing tourism has grown in recent years.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
