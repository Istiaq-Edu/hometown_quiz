=== LALMONIRHAT - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Lalmonirhat is a border district in which division?
A) Rajshahi
B) Rangpur
C) Sylhet
D) Mymensingh
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rangpur

[TRUE/FALSE] Lalmonirhat is known as the "District of the Red Earth" (though historically named after a Red Gem/Woman).
CORRECT ANSWER: False (Named after Lalmoni, a woman/gem, soil isn't particularly red like Madhupur).

[MCQ] "Mogholhat" Land Port (currently not fully operational/historic route) is in...
A) Lalmonirhat
B) Kurigram
C) Rangpur
D) Dinajpur
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Lalmonirhat

[MCQ] "Burimari" Land Port is a major land port in Lalmonirhat connecting...
A) India (and route to Bhutan/Nepal)
B) Myanmar
C) China
D) Thailand
CORRECT ANSWER: A) India

[TRUE/FALSE] Burimari is one of the busiest land ports for stone import.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Teesta Barrage" spans across Lalmonirhat and...
A) Nilphamari
B) Kurigram
C) Gaibandha
D) Bogra
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Nilphamari

[MCQ] The "Tin Bigha Corridor" connects the enclave...
A) Dahagram-Angarpota
B) Cooch Behar
C) Chitmahal
D) None
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Dahagram-Angarpota

[TRUE/FALSE] Travelers can visit the Tin Bigha Corridor.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river flows through Lalmonirhat?
A) Teesta and Dharla
B) Padma
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Teesta and Dharla

[MCQ] "Kakina Zamindar Bari" is a historic site.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] Lalmonirhat has a unique dialect closer to...
A) Rangpuri/Rajbanshi
B) Sylheti
C) Chatgaya
D) Barisali
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Rangpuri/Rajbanshi

[TRUE/FALSE] Agriculture (Tobacco, Maize, Potato) is a major livelihood.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Tobacco cultivation is very prominent in Lalmonirhat.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] "Bhawaiya" music is the soul of this region.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] "Vaoiya" festival is celebrated here.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Everyday Bangladesh

[MCQ] The distance from Dhaka to Lalmonirhat is approximately...
A) 350 km
B) 450 km (by road via Rangpur)
C) 550 km
D) 650 km
CORRECT ANSWER: B) 450 km

[MCQ] "Lalmoni Express" train connects Lalmonirhat to...
A) Dhaka
B) Chittagong
C) Sylhet
D) Khulna
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Dhaka

[TRUE/FALSE] Lalmonirhat has an airport (currently used by Air Force/STOL).
CORRECT ANSWER: True (Lalmonirhat Airport)

[MCQ] The main economic activities in Lalmonirhat include...
A) IT only
B) Agriculture (Tobacco, Maize), Stone crushing (at Burimari), and Trade
C) Ship building
D) Deep sea fishing
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agriculture, Stone crushing, and Trade

[TRUE/FALSE] Maize (Corn) production has revolutionized the local economy.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
