=== MAGURA - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Magura is located in which administrative division?
A) Rajshahi
B) Khulna
C) Dhaka
D) Barisal
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Khulna

[TRUE/FALSE] Magura was part of greater Jessore district.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river flows through Magura?
A) Padma
B) Madhumati and Nabaganga
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Madhumati and Nabaganga

[MCQ] "Mohammadpur" upazila is known for...
A) Mountains
B) Agricultural production
C) Desert
D) Beach
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural production

[TRUE/FALSE] Magura has flat terrain suitable for agriculture.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Shalikha" upazila is known for...
A) Hills
B) Agricultural areas
C) Tea gardens
D) Sea port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural areas

[MCQ] Magura has historic zamindari architecture.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] There are old temples and mosques in Magura.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Sreepur" upazila is known for...
A) Mountains
B) Agricultural production
C) Desert
D) Beach
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural production

[MCQ] The "Hazrat Khan Jahan Ali" route passes near Magura region.

CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Magura is connected to Jessore and Khulna by road.
