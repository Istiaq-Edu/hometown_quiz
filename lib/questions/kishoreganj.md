=== KISHOREGANJ - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Kishoreganj is known for its vast...
A) Mountains
B) Haor (wetland) areas
C) Deserts
D) Beaches
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Haor (wetland) areas

[TRUE/FALSE] "Nikli Haor" is one of the famous haors in Kishoreganj.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Kishoreganj is also known as the land of...
A) Hills
B) Folk music and culture
C) Deserts
D) Industry only
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Folk music and culture

[MCQ] The famous "Sholakia Eid Jamaat" (one of the largest Eid congregations) is held in...
A) Dhaka
B) Kishoreganj
C) Chittagong
D) Sylhet
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Kishoreganj

[TRUE/FALSE] The Sholakia Eidgah attracts lakhs of Muslims for Eid prayers.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river flows through Kishoreganj?
A) Padma
B) Meghna and Brahmaputra (Old)
C) Karnaphuli
D) Jamuna only
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Meghna and Brahmaputra (Old)

[MCQ] Kishoreganj was part of which greater district?
A) Dhaka
B) Mymensingh
C) Sylhet
D) Comilla
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Mymensingh

[TRUE/FALSE] Kishoreganj has a rich history of folk culture and music.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Egaro Sindur" is a historic site in...
A) Kishoreganj Sadar
B) Pakundia
C) Nikli
D) Mithamain
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Pakundia

[MCQ] "Mithamain" upazila is known for being...
A) Mountainous
B) Completely surrounded by water during monsoon (island upazila)
C) Desert-like
D) Industrial
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Completely surrounded by water during monsoon

[TRUE/FALSE] Some upazilas in Kishoreganj become inaccessible by road during floods.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Zilla Parishad" building in Kishoreganj is a...
A) Modern structure
B) Historic colonial-era building
C) Temple
D) Mosque
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Historic colonial-era building

[MCQ] "Austagram" upazila is known for...
A) Hills
B) Haor areas
C) Tea gardens
D) Sea port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Haor areas

[TRUE/FALSE] Kishoreganj has many historic temples and mosques.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The Chandrapur Shiva Temple is a significant religious site in Kishoreganj.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True
