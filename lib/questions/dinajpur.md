=== DINAJPUR - RE-CATEGORIZED AND EXPANDED QUIZ ===

Category: Places & History (Minimum 15 Questions)
Historical sites, landmarks, and the rich heritage of Dinajpur.

[MCQ] Dinajpur is famous for which ancient Hindu temple?
A) Dhakeshwari Temple
B) Kantaji Temple (Kantanagar Temple)
C) Jessoreswari Temple
D) Sitakunda Temple
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Kantaji Temple (Kantanagar Temple)

[TRUE/FALSE] The Kantaji Temple is renowned for its exquisite terracotta artwork depicting scenes from Hindu epics.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The Kantaji Temple was built during which dynasty/era?
A) Mughal era
B) Pala dynasty
C) Maharaja Pran Nath's reign (18th century)
D) British colonial period
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Maharaja Pran Nath's reign (18th century)

[MCQ] The "Ramsagar" is the largest...
A) River in Dinajpur
B) Man-made lake (dighi) in Bangladesh
C) Forest in the region
D) Temple complex
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Man-made lake (dighi) in Bangladesh

[TRUE/FALSE] Ramsagar was excavated by Raja Ram Nath in the 18th century.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Dinajpur Rajbari" (Dinajpur Palace) was the residence of...
A) Mughal emperors
B) Local Hindu Rajas/Zamindars
C) British governors
D) Buddhist monks
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Local Hindu Rajas/Zamindars

[MCQ] Which ancient archaeological site is located near Dinajpur?
A) Mahasthangarh
B) Bangarh (also known as Kotivarsha)
C) Paharpur
D) Mainamati
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bangarh (also known as Kotivarsha)

[TRUE/FALSE] Dinajpur was part of the ancient "Pundravardhana" region.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Nayabad Mosque" is a historic mosque located in which area of Dinajpur?
A) Dinajpur Sadar
B) Kaharole
C) Birol
D) Parbatipur
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Kaharole

[MCQ] Which river flows through Dinajpur district?
A) Padma
B) Punarbhaba and Atrai
C) Meghna
D) Jamuna
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Punarbhaba and Atrai

[TRUE/FALSE] Dinajpur is one of the largest districts in Bangladesh by area.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Swapna Puran" (Dream Garden) is a famous...
A) Temple
B) Eco-park and tourist spot
C) Museum
D) University
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Eco-park and tourist spot

[MCQ] The historic "Ghughu Danga" is associated with...
A) A famous battle
B) An ancient mound/archaeological site
C) A river port
D) A forest reserve
CORRECT ANSWER: B) An ancient mound/archaeological site

[TRUE/FALSE] Dinajpur was an important center during the Sena dynasty.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Singra Forest" near Dinajpur is known for...
A) Tea gardens
B) Sal trees and wildlife
C) Mangroves
D) Rubber plantations
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sal trees and wildlife

[MCQ] "Parbatipur" in Dinajpur is famous for its...
A) Temple
B) Railway junction
C) University
D) Airport
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Railway junction
