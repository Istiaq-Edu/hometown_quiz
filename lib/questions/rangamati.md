=== RANGAMATI - RE-CATEGORIZED AND EXPANDED QUIZ ===

Category: Places & History (Minimum 15 Questions)
Historical sites, landmarks, and the rich heritage of Rangamati.

[MCQ] Rangamati is located in which region of Bangladesh?
A) Barind Tract
B) Chittagong Hill Tracts (CHT)
C) Sylhet Division
D) Sundarbans
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Chittagong Hill Tracts (CHT)

[TRUE/FALSE] Rangamati is the largest district in Bangladesh by area.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Kaptai Lake" is the largest...
A) Natural lake in Bangladesh
B) Man-made lake in Bangladesh
C) River in Bangladesh
D) Reservoir in Southeast Asia
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Man-made lake in Bangladesh

[MCQ] Kaptai Lake was created by damming which river?
A) Padma
B) Karnaphuli River
C) Meghna
D) Jamuna
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Karnaphuli River

[TRUE/FALSE] The Kaptai Dam was built in the 1960s for hydroelectric power generation.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Chakma Rajbari" (Chakma King's Palace) is located in...
A) Kaptai
B) Rangamati town
C) Bandarban
D) Khagrachari
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rangamati town

[MCQ] Which indigenous community is the largest in Rangamati?
A) Marma
B) Chakma
C) Tripura
D) Garo
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Chakma

[TRUE/FALSE] The Chakma people have their own language and script.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Hanging Bridge" (Jhulonto Bridge) is a famous landmark in...
A) Kaptai
B) Rangamati town
C) Bandarban
D) Khagrachari
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rangamati town

[MCQ] "Shuvolong Waterfall" is located in...
A) Rangamati Sadar
B) Barkal upazila
C) Kaptai
D) Longadu
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Barkal upazila

[TRUE/FALSE] Rangamati is known for its scenic beauty with hills, lakes, and forests.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Rajban Vihara" is a famous...
A) Hindu temple
B) Buddhist monastery
C) Mosque
D) Church
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Buddhist monastery

[MCQ] The "Peda Ting Ting" is a scenic spot overlooking...
A) Kaptai Lake
B) Bay of Bengal
C) Padma River
D) Jamuna River
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Kaptai Lake

[TRUE/FALSE] The Chittagong Hill Tracts Peace Accord was signed in 1997 to end conflict in the region.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Bilaichari" is an upazila in Rangamati known for...
A) Urban development
B) Remote hills and indigenous villages
C) Sea port
D) Airport
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Remote hills and indigenous villages

[MCQ] The "Karnaphuli Paper Mills" is located in which area?
A) Rangamati
B) Chandraghona (near Kaptai)
C) Chittagong
D) Bandarban
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Chandraghona (near Kaptai)

Category: Culture & Traditions (Minimum 15 Questions)
Local customs, festivals, food, and the cultural identity of Rangamati.

[MCQ] The "Biju" festival is celebrated by which community?
A) Bengali Muslims
B) Chakma, Marma (as Sangrai), Tripura (as Boisuk)
C) Hindus only
D) Christians
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Chakma, Marma (as Sangrai), Tripura (as Boisuk)

[TRUE/FALSE] The Biju/Sangrai/Boisuk festival marks the Bengali New Year (Pahela Boishakh) for hill communities.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Traditional Chakma women wear...
A) Saree
B) "Pinon" and "Hadi" (traditional attire)
C) Salwar Kameez
D) Western clothes only
CORRECT ANSWER: B) "Pinon" and "Hadi" (traditional attire)

[MCQ] "Bamboo" and "Cane" crafts are traditional products of...
A) Dhaka
B) Rangamati and CHT
C) Sylhet
D) Khulna
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rangamati and CHT

[TRUE/FALSE] Weaving is a traditional craft among Chakma women.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Changi" is a traditional...
A) Food
B) Alcoholic rice beverage
C) Musical instrument
D) Dance
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Alcoholic rice beverage (rice beer)

[MCQ] Traditional Chakma cuisine often includes...
A) Seafood only
B) Bamboo shoots, dried fish, and hill vegetables
C) Pizza
D) Biryani only
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bamboo shoots, dried fish, and hill vegetables

[TRUE/FALSE] "Jhum" cultivation (shifting cultivation) is practiced in the hill tracts.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Marma" community celebrates which festival similar to Thai Songkran?
A) Eid
B) Sangrai (Water Festival)
C) Christmas
D) Durga Puja
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sangrai (Water Festival)

[MCQ] Traditional Marma men's attire includes...
A) Lungi and shirt
B) "Longyi" (similar to Myanmar)
C) Dhoti
D) Western suits
CORRECT ANSWER: B) "Longyi" (similar to Myanmar)

[TRUE/FALSE] The Tripura community has their own distinct culture and traditions.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Biddu" is a traditional Chakma...
A) Food
B) Musical instrument (flute)
C) Dance
D) Clothing
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Musical instrument (flute)

[MCQ] The indigenous communities of CHT practice which religion predominantly?
A) Islam
B) Buddhism and Christianity
C) Hinduism only
D) Animism only
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Buddhism and Christianity (with some traditional beliefs)

[TRUE/FALSE] Traditional houses in Rangamati are often built on stilts.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Pitha" made from rice flour is also popular among the hill communities.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rangamati
