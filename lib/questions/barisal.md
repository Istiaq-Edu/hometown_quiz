=== BARISAL - RE-CATEGORIZED AND EXPANDED QUIZ ===
Category: Places and History (Minimum 10 Questions)
This category focuses on specific landmarks, geographical features, historical events, and significant figures associated with Barisal's past and present.

[MCQ] Barisal is famously known as the...
A) Venice of Bengal
B) City of Silk
C) Queen of the East
D) Garden City
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Venice of Bengal
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Durga Sagar Dighi" is the largest man-made lake in southern Bangladesh, located in Barisal.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which river is the main river flowing past Barisal city?
A) Kirtankhola
B) Padma
C) Meghna
D) Jamuna
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Kirtankhola
[MCQ] The "Floating Guava Market" is a famous tourist attraction located in...
A) Bhola
B) Jhalokati (near Barisal)
C) Patuakhali
D) Pirojpur
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Jhalokati (near Barisal) - (specifically Bhimruli).
[MCQ] "Kuakata" sea beach, known for offering views of both sunrise and sunset, is located in which district of Barisal division?
A) Barisal
B) Bhola
C) Patuakhali
D) Barguna
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Patuakhali
[TRUE/FALSE] Sher-e-Bangla A.K. Fazlul Huq, a key political figure in Bengal, was from Barisal.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] The "Guthia Mosque" (Baitul Aman Jame Masjid) is famous for its...
A) Terracotta
B) Beautiful architecture and large pond
C) Golden dome
D) Ancient history
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Beautiful architecture and large pond
[MCQ] "Oxford of the East" was a title historically associated with which educational institution in Barisal (often compared to Dhaka University)?
A) Barisal Zilla School
B) Brojomohun College (BM College)
C) Barisal University
D) Sher-e-Bangla Medical College
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Brojomohun College (BM College)
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Bibir Pukur" is a historic pond located in the heart of Barisal city.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which island district in Barisal division is the largest island in Bangladesh?
A) Sandwip
B) Hatiya
C) Bhola
D) Kutubdia
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Bhola
[MCQ] The "Lebur Char" is a part of...
A) Kuakata Beach
B) Durga Sagar
C) Bhola Island
D) Monpura
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Kuakata Beach
[TRUE/FALSE] Barisal was historically a major grain-producing region, known as the "Granary of Bengal" (Dhan-Nodi-Khal, Ei tine Barisal).
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Monpura" island, famous for its scenic beauty and isolation, is located in...
A) Bhola
B) Patuakhali
C) Barguna
D) Pirojpur
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Bhola
[MCQ] The "Fatrar Chor" is a mangrove forest part of the Sundarbans located in...
A) Khulna
B) Kuakata (Patuakhali/Barguna)
C) Bhola
D) Barisal
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Kuakata (Patuakhali/Barguna)
[TRUE/FALSE] "Swarupkathi" is famous for its timber and boat-building industry.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which famous poetess was born in the village of Shaistabad in Barisal?
A) Begum Rokeya
B) Sufia Kamal

[MCQ] "Lalmohan" is an Upazila of...
