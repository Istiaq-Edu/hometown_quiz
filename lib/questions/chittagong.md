=== CHITTAGONG - RE-CATEGORIZED AND EXPANDED QUIZ ===
Category: Places and History (Minimum 10 Questions)
This category focuses on specific landmarks, geographical features, historical events, and significant figures associated with Chittagong's past and present.

[MCQ] Chittagong is known as the "Commercial Capital" of Bangladesh. What is its other popular nickname?
A) The City of Silk
B) The Port City
C) The City of Mosques
D) The Green City
CORRECT ANSWER: B) The Port City
[TRUE/FALSE] The Karnaphuli River is the most important river in Chittagong and the site of the country's largest seaport.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which famous beach in Chittagong is known as the longest natural sea beach in the world?
A) Patenga Beach
B) Parki Beach
C) Cox's Bazar Beach
D) Kattali Beach
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Cox's Bazar Beach (Note: While Cox's Bazar is in the Chittagong Division, Patenga is the main beach within Chittagong city itself. However, Cox's Bazar is the world famous one associated with the region).
[MCQ] The "Foy's Lake" is a man-made lake in Chittagong. Who was it named after?
A) A British railway engineer
B) A Mughal Subahdar
C) A local landlord
D) A famous poet
CORRECT ANSWER: A) A British railway engineer (Mr. Foy)
[MCQ] Which historical fort in Chittagong was used by the Mughals to defend against Arakanese and Portuguese pirates?
A) Lalbagh Fort
B) Anderkilla
C) Idrakpur Fort
D) Sonakanda Fort
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Anderkilla (Though now a place name, it was originally a fort area).
[TRUE/FALSE] The Chittagong Hill Tracts are the only extensive hill area in Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] The shrine of which famous Sufi saint is located in Chittagong, attracting thousands of visitors?
A) Shah Jalal
B) Shah Poran
C) Bayazid Bostami
D) Khan Jahan Ali
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Bayazid Bostami
[TRUE/FALSE] The Ethnological Museum in Chittagong is the only ethnological museum in Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] During the Liberation War of 1971, the declaration of independence was famously transmitted from which radio station?
A) Dhaka Betar Kendra
B) Kalurghat Radio Station
C) Rajshahi Radio Station
D) Khulna Radio Station
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Kalurghat Radio Station
[MCQ] The "War Cemetery" in Chittagong contains the graves of soldiers from which war?
A) World War I
B) World War II
C) Liberation War 1971
D) British-Mughal War
CORRECT ANSWER: B) World War II
[TRUE/FALSE] Patenga Beach is located near the mouth of the Karnaphuli River.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which Portuguese settlement in Chittagong was known as "Porto Grande"?
A) Dianga
B) Sandwip
C) Chittagong Port area
D) Firingi Bazar
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Chittagong Port area (The whole harbor was referred to as Porto Grande).
[MCQ] The "Batali Hill" is the highest hill in Chittagong city.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True
[MCQ] Which island is known as the "Lighthouse of the Bay of Bengal" and is part of Chittagong district?
A) Sandwip
B) Kutubdia
C) St. Martin's Island
D) Nijhum Dwip
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Kutubdia
[MCQ] "Chandranath Hill" is a famous pilgrimage site located in...
A) Rangamati
B) Sitakunda
C) Bandarban
D) Khagrachari
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sitakunda
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Zia Memorial Museum" is housed in the historic Old Circuit House of Chittagong.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Who was the leader of the "Chittagong Armoury Raid" in 1930?
A) Pritilata Waddedar
B) Masterda Surya Sen
C) Binod Bihari Chowdhury
D) Bagha Jatin
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Masterda Surya Sen
[MCQ] The "Naval Academy" of Bangladesh is located in...
A) Dhaka
B) Khulna
C) Chittagong (Patenga)
D) Cox's Bazar
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Chittagong (Patenga)
[TRUE/FALSE] "Guliakhali Beach" is famous for its mangrove-like environment and green grass carpet.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which river separates Chittagong city from the Boalkhali upazila?
A) Halda
B) Karnaphuli
C) Sangu
D) Matamuhuri
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Karnaphuli


Category: Culture and Traditions (Minimum 10 Questions)
This category covers festivals, arts, crafts, specific cultural practices, and aspects that define the unique identity and heritage of Chittagong and its people.

[MCQ] "Mezban" is a famous traditional feast of Chittagong. What is the main dish served?
A) Chicken Roast
B) Beef Curry
C) Mutton Biryani
D) Hilsa Fish
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Beef Curry
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Boli Khela" is a traditional wrestling competition held annually in Chittagong at Laldighi Maidan.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] The "Chittagonian" language (Chatgaya) is often considered a dialect of Bengali, but it is distinct enough to be considered a separate language by some linguists. It belongs to which language family?
A) Tibeto-Burman
B) Indo-Aryan
C) Dravidian
D) Austroasiatic
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Indo-Aryan
[MCQ] Which dried fish (Shutki) market in Chittagong is one of the largest in the country?
A) Asadganj
B) Reazuddin Bazar
C) Khatunganj
D) New Market
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Asadganj
[TRUE/FALSE] "Bela Biscuit" is a traditional and popular tea-time snack that originated in Chittagong.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] The tribal communities in the Chittagong Hill Tracts celebrate a major festival known as...
A) Pohela Boishakh
B) Vaisabi
C) Nabanna
D) Shakrain
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Vaisabi (Combination of Vaishuk, Sangrai, and Bizu).
[MCQ] Which musical instrument is commonly associated with the indigenous people of the Chittagong Hill Tracts?
A) Bamboo Flute
B) Harmonium
C) Tabla
D) Ektara
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Bamboo Flute
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Sampan" boat is a traditional symbol of Chittagong's riverine culture.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Kala Bhuna" is a famous dark-colored beef dish originating from...
A) Sylhet
B) Dhaka
C) Chittagong
D) Rajshahi
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Chittagong
[MCQ] Who is the legendary folk heroine of Chittagong whose love story with Mahipal is famous in local ballads?
A) Behula
B) Velua Sundari
C) Chandravati
D) Khona
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Velua Sundari
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Jobbar-er Boli Khela" takes place on the 12th of Boishakh every year.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Madhubhat" is a traditional fermented rice dessert popular in Chittagong.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True
[MCQ] Which famous rock band from Chittagong is known for songs like "Hasnuhana" and "Neela"?
A) LRB
B) Nagar Baul
C) Souls
D) Miles
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Souls (and also LRB/Ayub Bachchu had roots, but Souls is the quintessential Chittagong band).
[MCQ] The "Shemai" (Vermicelli) trade during Eid is historically centered in which market of Chittagong?
A) Chaktai
B) Khatunganj
C) Reazuddin Bazar
D) Pahartali
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Chaktai
[TRUE/FALSE] "Chatgaiya Rupon" is a popular comedy genre or character archetype representing the local dialect and humor.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Akhni" (a type of Biryani/Pulao) is a popular traditional dish in Chittagong.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True
