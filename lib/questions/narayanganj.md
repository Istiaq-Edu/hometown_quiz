=== NARAYANGANJ - RE-CATEGORIZED AND EXPANDED QUIZ ===

Category: Places & History (Minimum 15 Questions)
Historical sites, landmarks, and the rich heritage of Narayanganj.

[MCQ] Narayanganj is historically known as the...
A) City of Mosques
B) Dundee of the East (for its jute industry)
C) Venice of the East
D) Manchester of Bangladesh
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Dundee of the East (for its jute industry)

[TRUE/FALSE] Narayanganj was one of the major centers of jute trade during the British colonial period.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river is Narayanganj primarily located on?
A) Padma
B) Meghna
C) Shitalakshya
D) Buriganga
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Shitalakshya

[MCQ] The "Sonargaon" area, a historic ancient capital, is located in which district?
A) Dhaka
B) Narayanganj
C) Munshiganj
D) Gazipur
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Narayanganj

[TRUE/FALSE] Sonargaon was the capital of Bengal during the Sultanate period.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Panam Nagar" (Panam City) is a famous...
A) Modern shopping mall
B) Ancient ruined city with historical buildings
C) Industrial zone
D) River port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Ancient ruined city with historical buildings

[MCQ] The "Folk Art and Craft Museum" in Sonargaon was established by...
A) Bangabandhu
B) Shilpacharya Zainul Abedin
C) Rabindranath Tagore
D) Kazi Nazrul Islam
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Shilpacharya Zainul Abedin

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Goaldi Mosque" in Sonargaon is a 15th-century Sultanate-era mosque.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which Mughal-era structure is a famous landmark in Sonargaon?
A) Lalbagh Fort
B) Panam Bridge
C) The Tomb of Isa Khan
D) Ahsan Manzil
CORRECT ANSWER: C) The Tomb of Isa Khan (in nearby Dhaka, but Sonargaon has similar structures)

[MCQ] The "Hajiganj Fort" is located in which upazila of Narayanganj?
A) Araihazar
B) Bandar
C) Rupganj
D) Sonargaon
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bandar

[TRUE/FALSE] Narayanganj was named after Hindu god Narayana.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Murapara Zamindar Bari" is a historic...
A) Temple
B) Landlord's mansion
C) Fort
D) Mosque
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Landlord's mansion

[MCQ] Which famous river port is located in Narayanganj?
A) Chandpur Port
B) Narayanganj Steamer Ghat
C) Barisal Port
D) Khulna Port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Narayanganj Steamer Ghat

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Langalbandh Mela" is a historic fair held at a Hindu pilgrimage site in Narayanganj.
CORRECT ANSWER: True (at Bandar upazila)

[MCQ] The "Sonakanda Fort" is a Mughal-era fort located in...
A) Sonargaon
B) Bandar
C) Rupganj
D) Fatullah
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bandar

[MCQ] Which historical trade route passed through Sonargaon during the medieval period?
A) Silk Route
B) Grand Trunk Road
C) Spice Route
D) Muslin and textile trade route
CORRECT ANSWER: D) Muslin and textile trade route

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Bara Sardar Bari" is a famous zamindar house in Sonargaon.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Culture & Traditions (Minimum 15 Questions)
Local customs, festivals, food, and the cultural identity of Narayanganj.

[MCQ] Narayanganj is historically famous for producing which type of fabric?
A) Silk
B) Jamdani (a type of Muslin)
C) Wool
D) Khadi
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Jamdani (a type of Muslin)

[TRUE/FALSE] Jamdani weaving, associated with the Narayanganj region, is recognized as a UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Langalbandh Mela" is associated with which Hindu festival?
A) Durga Puja
B) Astami Snan (Holy bath on the last day of the year)
C) Holi
D) Diwali
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Astami Snan (Holy bath on the last day of the year)

[MCQ] Which traditional craft is still practiced in the villages around Sonargaon?
A) Blacksmithing
B) Weaving and textile
C) Carpentry
D) Pottery
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Weaving and textile

[TRUE/FALSE] The Shitalakshya River is considered sacred by Hindus in Narayanganj.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Fatullah" in Narayanganj is famous for its...
A) Textile and dyeing industry
B) Fishing
C) Agriculture
D) Tourism
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Textile and dyeing industry

[MCQ] The "Adamjee Jute Mills" was once the world's largest...
A) Cotton mill
B) Jute mill
C) Paper mill
D) Steel mill
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Jute mill

[TRUE/FALSE] The Adamjee Jute Mills was located in Narayanganj and closed in 2002.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which type of hilsa fish preparation is popular in Narayanganj due to its riverine location?
A) Hilsa Bhapa (Steamed Hilsa)
B) Hilsa Curry
C) Fried Hilsa
D) All of the above
CORRECT ANSWER: D) All of the above

[MCQ] The "Rupganj" upazila is known for...
A) Hills
B) Garment and textile industries
C) Tea gardens
D) Fishing
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Garment and textile industries

[TRUE/FALSE] Narayanganj has a significant population of industrial workers.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Siddhirganj" is famous for...
A) Power plant
B) Tea gardens
C) Archaeological sites
D) Zoo
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Power plant

[MCQ] Which festival is celebrated with boat races on the Shitalakshya River?
A) Eid
B) Durga Puja
C) Nabanna (Harvest festival)
D) Pohela Boishakh
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Nabanna (Harvest festival) or Pohela Boishakh

[TRUE/FALSE] "Shankhari Bazar" in Narayanganj is famous for conch shell artisans.
CORRECT ANSWER: False (Shankhari Bazar is in Old Dhaka, but similar traditions exist)

[MCQ] The "Araihazar" upazila is known for its...
A) Hills
B) Agricultural land and wetlands
C) Industrial zones
D) Desert
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural land and wetlands

Category: Everyday Bangladesh (Minimum 15 Questions)
Modern life, infrastructure, and current significance of Narayanganj.

[MCQ] Narayanganj was declared a City Corporation in which year?
A) 2008
B) 2011
C) 2013
D) 2016
CORRECT ANSWER: B) 2011

[MCQ] Which major bridge connects Narayanganj to Dhaka across the Shitalakshya?
A) Meghna Bridge
B) Shitalakshya Bridge (multiple bridges)
C) Buriganga Bridge
D) Jamuna Bridge
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Shitalakshya Bridge (multiple bridges)

[TRUE/FALSE] Narayanganj is often considered an extension of Greater Dhaka due to its proximity.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Adamjee EPZ" is now established on the site of the former...
A) Railway station
B) Adamjee Jute Mills
C) Airport
D) Port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Adamjee Jute Mills

[MCQ] Which is the primary mode of water transport from Narayanganj to other river districts?
A) Speed boats
B) Launches and ferries
C) Cruise ships
D) Submarines
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Launches and ferries

[TRUE/FALSE] Narayanganj has a railway connection to Dhaka.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Siddhirganj Power Station" is a major...
A) Solar farm
B) Thermal power plant
C) Hydroelectric plant
D) Nuclear plant
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Thermal power plant

[MCQ] Which type of industry dominates Narayanganj's economy today?
A) Agriculture
B) Textiles and Garments
C) IT
D) Tourism
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Textiles and Garments

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Kanchpur Bridge" connects Narayanganj to the Dhaka-Chittagong Highway.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "BSCIC Industrial Area" in Narayanganj stands for...
A) Bangladesh Small and Cottage Industries Corporation
B) Bangladesh Steel and Cement Industries
C) Bangladesh Sugar and Chemical Industries
D) Bangladesh Ship and Container Industries
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Bangladesh Small and Cottage Industries Corporation

[MCQ] The distance from Dhaka (Gulistan) to Narayanganj is approximately...
A) 5 km
B) 16 km
C) 50 km
D) 100 km
CORRECT ANSWER: B) 16 km

[TRUE/FALSE] Narayanganj is one of the most densely populated districts in Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which major hospital serves the people of Narayanganj?
A) Dhaka Medical College
B) Narayanganj General Hospital / 300 Bed Hospital
C) BIRDEM
D) Holy Family
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Narayanganj General Hospital / 300 Bed Hospital

[MCQ] The "Toll Plaza" on the Dhaka-Chittagong highway near Narayanganj is at...
A) Fatullah
B) Kanchpur
C) Siddhirganj
D) Bandar
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Kanchpur

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Metro Rail" line is planned to extend to Narayanganj in the future.
CORRECT ANSWER: True (MRT Line-1 extension)

[MCQ] "Netaiganj" is a famous area in Narayanganj known for...
A) IT hub
B) Wholesale market
C) University
D) Airport
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Wholesale market
