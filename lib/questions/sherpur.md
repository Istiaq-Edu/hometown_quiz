=== SHERPUR - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Sherpur is located in which administrative division?
A) Dhaka
B) Mymensingh
C) Sylhet
D) Rangpur
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Mymensingh

[TRUE/FALSE] Sherpur was part of greater Mymensingh district.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which hill range borders the northern part of Sherpur?
A) Chittagong Hill Tracts
B) Garo Hills (Meghalaya, India)
C) Sylhet Hills
D) Madhupur Garh
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Garo Hills

[MCQ] "Gajni" is a famous...
A) River
B) Forest and picnic spot
C) Temple
D) Market
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Forest and picnic spot

[TRUE/FALSE] Sherpur shares a border with India (Meghalaya).
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river flows through Sherpur?
A) Padma
B) Brahmaputra and Kangsha
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Brahmaputra and Kangsha

[MCQ] "Madhutila Eco Park" is located in...
A) Sherpur Sadar
B) Nalitabari
C) Jhenaigati
D) Sreebardi
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Nalitabari

[TRUE/FALSE] The Garo indigenous community lives in parts of Sherpur.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Jhenaigati" upazila is known for...
A) Beach
B) Hilly terrain and indigenous communities
C) Desert
D) Sea port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Hilly terrain and indigenous communities

[MCQ] The "Pogla Mazar" is a religious site in Sherpur.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Sherpur has areas with hilly terrain near the Indian border.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Nakla" upazila is known for...
A) Mountains
B) Agricultural production
C) Desert
D) Beach
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural production

[MCQ] The "Hajong" community also lives in parts of Sherpur.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Sherpur has diverse geography including plains and hilly areas.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
