=== BOGRA - RE-CATEGORIZED AND EXPANDED QUIZ ===

Category: Places & History (Minimum 15 Questions)
Historical sites, landmarks, and the rich heritage of Bogra.

[MCQ] Bogra is famous for the ancient archaeological site called...
A) Paharpur
B) Mahasthangarh
C) Mainamati
D) Wari-Bateshwar
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Mahasthangarh

[TRUE/FALSE] Mahasthangarh is considered one of the earliest urban archaeological sites in Bangladesh, dating back to the 3rd century BCE.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Mahasthangarh was the ancient capital of which kingdom?
A) Pundravardhana
B) Samatata
C) Vanga
D) Harikela
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Pundravardhana

[MCQ] The "Parasuram's Palace" (Parashuramer Prasad) is an archaeological site located in...
A) Bogra Sadar
B) Mahasthangarh
C) Shibganj
D) Sherpur
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Mahasthangarh

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Govinda Bhita Temple" is an ancient Buddhist temple site in Mahasthangarh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Vasu Bihar" is a famous ancient...
A) Hindu temple
B) Buddhist monastery
C) Jain temple
D) Mosque
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Buddhist monastery

[MCQ] The "Khodar Pathar Bhita" is believed to be the site of...
A) An ancient mosque
B) A Hindu temple dedicated to Shiva
C) A Buddhist stupa
D) A Jain temple
CORRECT ANSWER: C) A Buddhist stupa (or possibly a temple)

[TRUE/FALSE] The Karatoya River flows past Mahasthangarh and was considered sacred in ancient times.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Gokul Medh" is a high mound in Mahasthangarh believed to be a...
A) Burial site
B) Temple or monastery
C) Palace
D) Market
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Temple or monastery

[MCQ] The Bogra Museum primarily displays artifacts from...
A) British colonial period
B) Mahasthangarh and surrounding archaeological sites
C) Mughal period
D) Modern art
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Mahasthangarh and surrounding archaeological sites

[TRUE/FALSE] Bogra was named after Shah Sultan Bogra, a Sufi saint.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which district does Bogra belong to administratively?
A) Dhaka Division
B) Rajshahi Division
C) Rangpur Division
D) Sylhet Division
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rajshahi Division

[MCQ] The "Mazar of Shah Sultan Balkhi Mahisawar" is a famous shrine in...
A) Bogra Sadar
B) Mahasthangarh
C) Shibganj
D) Gabtali
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Mahasthangarh

[TRUE/FALSE] Shah Sultan Balkhi Mahisawar is believed to have arrived in Bengal riding a giant fish (Mahisawar = Fish Rider).
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Shibganj" upazila is famous for...
A) Mango orchards
B) Jute mills
C) Hills
D) Sea beaches
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Mango orchards

[MCQ] The "Santahar Junction" is a famous...
A) Bus terminal
B) Railway junction
C) Airport
D) River port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Railway junction

[TRUE/FALSE] Bogra was an important center during the Pala dynasty of Bengal.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Culture & Traditions (Minimum 15 Questions)
Local customs, festivals, food, and the cultural identity of Bogra.

[MCQ] Bogra is nationally famous for which type of sweet/yogurt?
A) Rosogolla
B) Bogurar Doi (Bogra Curd/Yogurt)
C) Chomchom
D) Monda
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bogurar Doi (Bogra Curd/Yogurt)

[TRUE/FALSE] Bogra's curd (doi) is known for its thick, creamy texture and unique taste.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The famous shop "Sheronya" is known for making the best...
A) Biryani
B) Doi (Curd)
C) Sweets
D) Tea
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Doi (Curd)

[MCQ] Which traditional festival is celebrated at Mahasthangarh with a large fair?
A) Durga Puja
B) Pohela Boishakh and religious fairs
C) Christmas
D) Eid
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Pohela Boishakh and religious fairs

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Mahasthaner Mela" is an annual fair held at the archaeological site.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which folk dance is popular in the Bogra region?
A) Manipuri
B) Gambhira
C) Jhumur
D) Kathak
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Gambhira (popular in the greater Rajshahi region)

[MCQ] "Mango" is a major crop in Bogra. Which variety is famous?
A) Langra
B) Fazli
C) Himsagar
D) All of the above
CORRECT ANSWER: D) All of the above

[TRUE/FALSE] Bogra is part of the famous mango belt of Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which traditional craft is practiced in some rural areas of Bogra?
A) Jamdani
B) Silk weaving
C) Bamboo and cane craft
D) Conch shell work
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Bamboo and cane craft

[MCQ] The "Bazaar" or market culture in Bogra features weekly "Hat" (village markets). Which day is famous for large hats?
A) Only Friday
B) Various days in different areas
C) Only Sunday
D) Only Monday
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Various days in different areas

[TRUE/FALSE] Rice is the staple food in Bogra, often accompanied by fish and vegetables.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Pitha" (rice cakes) are popular in Bogra, especially during which season?
A) Summer
B) Monsoon
C) Winter
D) Autumn
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Winter

[MCQ] Which language/dialect is spoken in Bogra?
A) Standard Bengali with Bogra dialect
B) Sylheti
C) Chittagonian
D) Rangpuri
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Standard Bengali with Bogra dialect

[TRUE/FALSE] Bogra has a tradition of terracotta art, influenced by the nearby Kantaji Temple.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Jatra" (folk theatre) is a popular form of entertainment in rural Bogra.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True
