=== SHARIATPUR - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Shariatpur is located in which administrative division?
A) Rajshahi
B) Dhaka
C) Khulna
D) Barisal
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Dhaka

[TRUE/FALSE] Shariatpur is named after Haji Shariatullah, a famous Islamic reformer.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Haji Shariatullah was the founder of which movement?
A) Khilafat Movement
B) Faraizi Movement
C) Non-Cooperation Movement
D) Swadeshi Movement
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Faraizi Movement

[MCQ] Which river forms the boundary of Shariatpur?
A) Jamuna
B) Padma and Meghna
C) Karnaphuli
D) Teesta
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Padma and Meghna

[TRUE/FALSE] Shariatpur benefits from the Padma Bridge connectivity.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Naria" upazila is known for...
A) Mountains
B) Ferry terminal and river access
C) Desert
D) Tea gardens
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Ferry terminal and river access

[MCQ] The confluence of Padma and Meghna rivers is near Shariatpur.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Shariatpur has significant char (riverine island) areas.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Bhedarganj" upazila is known for...
A) Hills
B) Agricultural production
C) Tea gardens
D) Sea port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural production

[MCQ] Shariatpur was part of which greater district?
A) Dhaka
B) Faridpur
C) Barisal
D) Comilla
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Faridpur

[TRUE/FALSE] River erosion is a major challenge in Shariatpur.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Damudya" upazila is known for...
A) Mountains
B) Agricultural areas
C) Desert
D) Beach
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural areas
