=== RAJBARI - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Rajbari is located in which administrative division?
A) Rajshahi
B) Dhaka
C) Khulna
D) Barisal
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Dhaka

[TRUE/FALSE] Rajbari was part of greater Faridpur district.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river flows near Rajbari?
A) Jamuna
B) Padma and Hisna
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Padma and Hisna

[MCQ] "Goalanda" is famous as a historic...
A) Temple
B) River port and railway junction
C) Airport
D) Hill station
CORRECT ANSWER: B) River port and railway junction

[TRUE/FALSE] Goalanda was an important steamer station during British rule.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Goalanda Ghat" connects to...
A) Chittagong
B) Northern and southern Bangladesh via river
C) Sylhet
D) Rangpur
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Northern and southern Bangladesh

[MCQ] "Baliakandi" upazila is known for...
A) Mountains
B) Agricultural production
C) Desert
D) Beach
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural production

[TRUE/FALSE] Rajbari has historic railway connections.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Pangsha" upazila is known for...
A) Hills
B) Agricultural areas
C) Tea gardens
D) Sea port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural areas

[MCQ] Rajbari has flat terrain with rivers.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Date palm cultivation is practiced in Rajbari.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Kalukhali" upazila is known for...
A) Mountains
B) Agricultural production
C) Desert
D) Beach
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural production

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] Rajbari is known for which traditional product?
A) Tea
B) Khejur Gur (date palm jaggery)
C) Coffee
D) Rubber
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Khejur Gur

[TRUE/FALSE] Date palm jaggery production is a significant cottage industry.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Pitha" and sweets are especially popular during...
A) Summer
B) Winter (with date palm jaggery)
C) Monsoon
D) Autumn
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Winter

[MCQ] The main occupation in rural Rajbari is...
A) IT
B) Agriculture
C) Mining
D) Heavy industry
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agriculture

[TRUE/FALSE] Fishing is practiced in Rajbari's rivers.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which crops are grown in Rajbari?
A) Tea only
B) Rice, jute, and vegetables
C) Rubber
D) Coffee
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rice, jute, and vegetables

[TRUE/FALSE] Traditional boat making is practiced in Rajbari.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Everyday Bangladesh

[MCQ] The distance from Dhaka to Rajbari is approximately...
A) 50 km
B) 80 km
C) 110 km
D) 150 km
CORRECT ANSWER: C) 110 km

[MCQ] The Padma Bridge has improved connectivity to Rajbari.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Rajbari has road and railway connectivity.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The main economic activities in Rajbari include...
A) IT only
B) Agriculture and trade
C) Heavy industry
D) Gold mining
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agriculture and trade

[MCQ] Rajbari's economy is primarily based on...
A) Heavy industry
B) Agriculture
C) IT
D) Mining
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agriculture

[TRUE/FALSE] Rajbari is developing with improved infrastructure.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
