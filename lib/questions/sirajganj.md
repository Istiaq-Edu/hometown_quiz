=== SIRAJGANJ - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Sirajganj is famous for which major infrastructure?
A) Padma Bridge
B) Bangabandhu Bridge (Jamuna Bridge)
C) Karnaphuli Tunnel
D) Hardinge Bridge
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bangabandhu Bridge (Jamuna Bridge)

[TRUE/FALSE] The West side of the Bangabandhu Bridge ends in Sirajganj.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Sirajganj is known as the "Gateway to..."
A) South Bengal
B) North Bengal
C) East Bengal
D) Cox's Bazar
CORRECT ANSWER: B) North Bengal

[MCQ] Which river flows along Sirajganj?
A) Padma
B) Jamuna
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Jamuna

[TRUE/FALSE] Sirajganj experiences riverbank erosion from the Jamuna river.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Rabindra Kachari Bari" (Revenue Office of Tagore) is located in which upazila?
A) Sirajganj Sadar
B) Shahjadpur
C) Ullahpara
D) Belkuchi
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Shahjadpur

[MCQ] Rabindranath Tagore stayed in Shahjadpur and wrote parts of his work.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] "China Badh" (China Dam) or Crossbar Dam is a popular spot in...
A) Dhaka
B) Sirajganj (on Jamuna river)
C) Chittagong
D) Sylhet
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sirajganj

[TRUE/FALSE] Sirajganj was once a major river port for jute trade.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Navaratna Temple" (Hatikumrul) is a historic...
A) Mosque
B) Hindu Temple
C) Buddhist Monastery
D) Church
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Hindu Temple

[MCQ] "Ullahpara" is a major...
A) Sea port
B) Railway junction
C) Airport
D) Hill station
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Railway junction

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] Sirajganj is famous for which handloom product?
A) Jamdani
B) Tangail Saree
C) Sirajganj Handloom Saree and Lungi
D) Muslin
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Sirajganj Handloom Saree and Lungi

[TRUE/FALSE] The weaving industry (Tat Shilpa) is very significant in Belkuchi and Shahjadpur.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Sirajganj is also famous for which dairy product?
A) Cheese
B) Ghee (Clarified Butter) and Milk (Milk Vita hub)
C) Chocolate
D) Ice cream
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Ghee and Milk

[TRUE/FALSE] Baghabari in Sirajganj is a major milk collection hub.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The Nobel laureate Rabindranath Tagore had an estate in...
A) Kushtia only
B) Shahjadpur, Sirajganj
C) Dhaka
D) Sylhet
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Shahjadpur, Sirajganj

[MCQ] "Pitha" festivals are common in winter.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Bhatiali and Bhawaiya songs are popular in the riverine areas.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Everyday Bangladesh

[MCQ] The distance from Dhaka to Sirajganj is approximately...
A) 80 km
B) 130 km
C) 200 km
D) 300 km
CORRECT ANSWER: B) 130 km

[MCQ] "Enayetpur" is famous for...
A) Tea gardens
B) Enayetpur Khwaja Enayetpuri (R) Darbar Sharif and Medical College
C) Sea beach
D) Mountains
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Darbar Sharif and Medical College

[TRUE/FALSE] Sirajganj has direct rail connection to Dhaka.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The main economic activities in Sirajganj include...
A) IT only
B) Agriculture, weaving (handloom), and fishing
C) Mining
D) Tourism only
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agriculture, weaving (handloom), and fishing

[MCQ] "Baghabari" is a...
A) River port and power station hub
B) Airport
C) Train station
D) Bus terminal
CORRECT ANSWER: A) River port and power station hub

[TRUE/FALSE] The Jamuna Multipurpose Bridge allows both rail and road traffic.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
