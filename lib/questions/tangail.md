=== TANGAIL - RE-CATEGORIZED AND EXPANDED QUIZ ===

Category: Places & History (Minimum 15 Questions)
Historical sites, landmarks, and the rich heritage of Tangail.

[MCQ] Tangail is historically famous for which textile?
A) Jamdani
B) Tangail Saree
C) Muslin
D) Silk
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Tangail Saree

[TRUE/FALSE] The Tangail Saree is known for its distinctive weaving pattern and soft texture.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Atia Mosque" is a historic mosque built during...
A) Sultanate period
B) Mughal period (17th century)
C) British period
D) Pakistan period
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Mughal period (17th century)

[MCQ] The "Mohera Zamindar Bari" is a famous...
A) Temple
B) Historic landlord's mansion
C) Fort
D) Museum
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Historic landlord's mansion

[TRUE/FALSE] The Mohera Zamindar Bari features a blend of European and Bengali architectural styles.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Madhupur Garh" (Madhupur Forest) is known for...
A) Tea gardens
B) Sal forest and Garo community
C) Mangroves
D) Hills
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sal forest and Garo community

[MCQ] Which famous Bengali politician was born in Tangail?
A) Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman
B) Maulana Abdul Hamid Khan Bhashani
C) Ziaur Rahman
D) A.K. Fazlul Huq
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Maulana Abdul Hamid Khan Bhashani

[TRUE/FALSE] Maulana Bhashani's mazar (tomb) is located in Santosh, Tangail.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Pakutia Zamindar Bari" is another historic...
A) Temple
B) Landlord's mansion in Tangail
C) Fort
D) Mosque
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Landlord's mansion in Tangail

[MCQ] Which river flows along the border of Tangail district?
A) Padma
B) Jamuna (Brahmaputra)
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Jamuna (Brahmaputra)

[TRUE/FALSE] Tangail was part of the ancient "Mymensingh" region.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Nagarpur Upazila" is famous for its...
A) Saree weaving
B) Hills
C) Tea gardens
D) Fishing
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Saree weaving

[MCQ] The "Dhanbari Nawab Bari" is a historic palace located in...
A) Tangail Sadar
B) Dhanbari upazila
C) Madhupur
D) Gopalpur
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Dhanbari upazila

[TRUE/FALSE] The Kumudini Hospital and Kumudini Women's Medical College are located in Tangail.
CORRECT ANSWER: True (in Mirzapur)

[MCQ] The "Santosh" area is significant for...
A) Industrial zone
B) Bhashani's legacy and Mazar
C) Tea gardens
D) Port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bhashani's legacy and Mazar

[MCQ] The "Delduar" upazila is known for...
A) Saree production
B) Hills
C) Sea port
D) Airport
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Saree production
