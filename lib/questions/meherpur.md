=== MEHERPUR - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Meherpur is significant in Bangladesh's history because...
A) It has mountains
B) The Mujibnagar government was formed here in 1971
C) It is the capital
D) It has the largest port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) The Mujibnagar government was formed here in 1971

[TRUE/FALSE] Mujibnagar was the first capital of independent Bangladesh during the Liberation War.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Mujibnagar Memorial" is located in which upazila?
A) Meherpur Sadar
B) Mujibnagar
C) Gangni
D) None
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Mujibnagar

[MCQ] Meherpur is located in which administrative division?
A) Rajshahi
B) Khulna
C) Dhaka
D) Barisal
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Khulna

[TRUE/FALSE] Meherpur is the smallest district in Bangladesh by area.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river flows through Meherpur?
A) Padma
B) Bhairab and Mathabhanga
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bhairab and Mathabhanga

[MCQ] Meherpur shares a border with which country?
A) Myanmar
B) India
C) Nepal
D) Bhutan
CORRECT ANSWER: B) India

[TRUE/FALSE] Meherpur is a border district.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Gangni" upazila is known for...
A) Mountains
B) Agricultural production
C) Desert
D) Beach
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural production

[MCQ] The historic "Amjhupi Nilkuthi" (Indigo factory) is in Meherpur.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Meherpur has historic significance from the indigo plantation era.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[TRUE/FALSE] Date palm jaggery is produced in Meherpur.

A) Summer
B) Winter

D) Autumn

[MCQ] The main occupation in rural Meherpur is...
A) IT

[TRUE/FALSE] Traditional folk culture is part of Meherpur's heritage.

A) 200 km
