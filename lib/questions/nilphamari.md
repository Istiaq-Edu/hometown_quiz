=== NILPHAMARI - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Nilphamari is famous for the "Nilsagar" which is a...
A) Large historic pond/lake (dighi)
B) Sea
C) River
D) Mountain
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Large historic pond/lake

[TRUE/FALSE] The name "Nilphamari" is derived from "Nil" (Indigo) farming ("Nil Khamar").
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Saidpur" in Nilphamari is famous for...
A) Saidpur Railway Workshop and Airport
B) Sea port
C) Tea gardens
D) Coal mines
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Saidpur Railway Workshop and Airport

[MCQ] Saidpur is known as a commercial hub of the North.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] "Teesta Barrage" project has some parts/canals in Nilphamari.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] "Uttara EPZ" (Export Processing Zone) is located in which district?
A) Dhaka
B) Nilphamari (Uttara EPZ, Sanggalshi)
C) Rangpur
D) Bogra
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Nilphamari

[TRUE/FALSE] Uttara EPZ has created employment opportunities in the region.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Chini Mosque" (Chinese Tile Mosque) is a historic mosque in...
A) Saidpur
B) Nilphamari Sadar
C) Jaldhaka
D) Dimla
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Saidpur

[MCQ] Which river flows through Nilphamari?
A) Teesta
B) Padma
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Teesta

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] Nilphamari has a significant population of "Bihari" (Urdu-speaking) community in...
A) Jaldhaka
B) Saidpur

[TRUE/FALSE] Saidpur is known for its distinct food culture (e.g., Kababs).

[MCQ] "Bhawaiya" is a popular folk song genre here.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] Bamboo and Cane crafts are traditional in some areas.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] Agriculture is the backbone of the rural economy.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Everyday Bangladesh

[MCQ] The distance from Dhaka to Nilphamari is approximately...
A) 300 km
B) 400 km
C) 500 km
D) 600 km
CORRECT ANSWER: B) 400 km

[MCQ] Saidpur Airport is one of the busiest domestic airports in North Bengal.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] There is direct train connectivity to Dhaka (e.g., Nilsagar Express).
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Nilsagar Express" is named after...
A) The historic pond Nilsagar
B) The blue sky
C) The river
D) A person
CORRECT ANSWER: A) The historic pond Nilsagar

[MCQ] The main economic activities in Nilphamari include...
A) IT only
B) Agriculture, Railway workshop, Small industries (EPZ), and Trade
C) Ship building
D) Mining
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agriculture, Railway workshop, Small industries, and Trade

[TRUE/FALSE] Domar upazila is known for "Domar Sand" (used in construction, though actually from nearby riverbeds).
CORRECT ANSWER: True (It's famous for high quality bold sand).
