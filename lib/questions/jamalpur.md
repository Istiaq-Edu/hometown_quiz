=== JAMALPUR - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Jamalpur is named after...
A) A Mughal emperor
B) Shah Jamal, a Sufi saint
C) A British officer
D) A local zamindar
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Shah Jamal, a Sufi saint

[TRUE/FALSE] The mazar (shrine) of Shah Jamal is located in Jamalpur.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river flows along Jamalpur district?
A) Padma
B) Brahmaputra (Jamuna) and Old Brahmaputra
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Brahmaputra (Jamuna) and Old Brahmaputra

[MCQ] The "Dewanganj" upazila is known for...
A) Hills
B) Chars (riverine islands) and agriculture
C) Tea gardens
D) Sea port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Chars (riverine islands) and agriculture

[TRUE/FALSE] Jamalpur has significant char lands formed by the Brahmaputra River.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Lauchapara Picnic Spot" is located in...
A) Jamalpur Sadar
B) Bakshiganj
C) Melandaha
D) Islampur
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bakshiganj

[MCQ] Jamalpur was part of which district before becoming independent?
A) Dhaka
B) Mymensingh

D) Bogra
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Mymensingh

[TRUE/FALSE] Jamalpur has historic mosques from the medieval period.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Garo Hills" border is near which upazila of Jamalpur?
A) Jamalpur Sadar
B) Bakshiganj
C) Dewanganj
D) Melandaha
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bakshiganj

[MCQ] Which archaeological site is found in Jamalpur region?
A) Mahasthangarh
B) Various mounds and historic sites
C) Paharpur
D) Mainamati
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Various mounds and historic sites

[TRUE/FALSE] Jamalpur has a connection to the Garo indigenous community.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Jamalpur Railway Station" is on which rail line?

[MCQ] "Islampur" upazila is known for its...
A) Hills
B) Historic sites and agriculture

[TRUE/FALSE] The Old Brahmaputra River flows through Jamalpur.

A) As a naval base

[MCQ] "Sarishabari" upazila was formerly part of Jamalpur but is now in...
