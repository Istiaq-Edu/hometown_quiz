=== RAJSHAHI - RE-CATEGORIZED AND EXPANDED QUIZ ===
Category: Places and History (Minimum 10 Questions)
This category focuses on specific landmarks, geographical features, historical events, and significant figures associated with Rajshahi's past and present.

[MCQ] Rajshahi is historically known as "Rampur Boalia". It is famously nicknamed the...
A) City of Mosques
B) Silk City
C) Port City
D) Garden City
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Silk City
[TRUE/FALSE] The Varendra Research Museum in Rajshahi is the oldest museum in Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which mighty river flows along the southern border of Rajshahi city?
A) Padma
B) Jamuna
C) Meghna
D) Teesta
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Padma
[MCQ] The "Puthia Temple Complex" is a famous cluster of historic Hindu temples located in Rajshahi. Which architectural style is prominent there?
A) Mughal
B) Terracotta
C) Gothic
D) Modern
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Terracotta (specifically Bengal terracotta style).
[MCQ] "Bagha Mosque" is a historical mosque in Rajshahi known for its...
A) Golden dome
B) Terracotta ornamentation
C) White marble structure
D) Tallest minaret
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Terracotta ornamentation
[TRUE/FALSE] Rajshahi University is the second oldest university in Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Who was the famous indigo planter who built the "Kuthi" (now part of Rajshahi College) in the 19th century?
A) Lord Curzon
B) Robert Watson
C) William Carey
D) Warren Hastings
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Robert Watson (It was a 'Baro Kuthi' for indigo trade).
[MCQ] The "Somapura Mahavihara" (Paharpur), a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is located in the greater Rajshahi division (Naogaon). It was a famous...
A) Hindu Temple
B) Buddhist Monastery
C) Mughal Fort
D) Sufi Shrine
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Buddhist Monastery
[TRUE/FALSE] The "T-Groin" or "T-Badh" is a popular riverside recreational spot in Rajshahi.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which historical figure is associated with the "Choto Sona Mosque" (Small Golden Mosque) in the Rajshahi division (Chapainawabganj)?
A) Sultan Alauddin Husain Shah
B) Shaista Khan
C) Isa Khan
D) Sultan Jalaluddin Muhammad Shah
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Sultan Alauddin Husain Shah (Built during his reign by Wali Muhammad).
[MCQ] The "Martyr's Memorial" (Smriti Amlan) in Rajshahi city commemorates...
A) The Language Movement
B) The Liberation War
C) Both the Language Movement and Liberation War
D) The 1969 Mass Uprising
CORRECT ANSWER: B) The Liberation War
[TRUE/FALSE] Rajshahi was one of the first municipalities established in Bengal during the British Raj.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Mahasthangarh", the oldest archaeological site in Bangladesh, is located in which district of Rajshahi division?
A) Rajshahi
B) Bogra
C) Naogaon
D) Natore
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bogra
[MCQ] The "Uttara Ganabhaban" (Northern People's House) in Natore was originally the palace of...
A) The Dighapatia Raj
B) The Puthia Raj
C) The Natore Raj
D) The Rani Bhabani
CORRECT ANSWER: A) The Dighapatia Raj
[TRUE/FALSE] "Behula's Basor Ghar" is a legendary site associated with Mahasthangarh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which famous queen of Natore was known for her philanthropy and administration?
A) Rani Rashmoni
B) Rani Bhabani
C) Rani Padmini
D) Rani Laxmibai
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rani Bhabani
[MCQ] The "Kusumba Mosque" in Naogaon is depicted on which Bangladeshi currency note?
A) 5 Taka
B) 10 Taka
C) 50 Taka
D) 100 Taka
CORRECT ANSWER: A) 5 Taka
[TRUE/FALSE] Rajshahi College is one of the oldest and most prestigious colleges in Bangladesh, established in 1873.
CORRECT ANSWER: True


Category: Culture and Traditions (Minimum 10 Questions)
This category covers festivals, arts, crafts, specific cultural practices, and aspects that define the unique identity and heritage of Rajshahi and its people.

[MCQ] Rajshahi is famous for producing the finest quality of which fruit?
A) Jackfruit
B) Mango
C) Litchi
D) Pineapple
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Mango
[TRUE/FALSE] "Gambhira" is a traditional folk song and dance form of Rajshahi performed by a 'Nana' (grandfather) and 'Nati' (grandson).
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which type of silk is Rajshahi most famous for?
A) Muga Silk
B) Eri Silk
C) Mulberry Silk (Rajshahi Silk)
D) Tussar Silk
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Mulberry Silk (Rajshahi Silk)
[MCQ] "Kalai Ruti" (bread made from black gram flour) is a popular traditional food of which region?
A) Sylhet
B) Chittagong
C) Rajshahi (specifically Chapainawabganj/Rajshahi)
D) Barisal
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Rajshahi
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Alkap" is a rural folk drama performance popular in the Rajshahi region.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Fazli" is a famous variety of...
A) Silk
B) Mango
C) Rice
D) Sweet
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Mango
[MCQ] The "Santal" community, one of the largest indigenous groups in the Rajshahi division, celebrates a harvest festival called...
A) Sohrai
B) Baha
C) Karam
D) All of the above
CORRECT ANSWER: D) All of the above (Sohrai is a major one).
[TRUE/FALSE] Rajshahi is known for its high-quality "Roshomalai" (though Comilla is also famous, Rajshahi has its own distinct sweets like Raghobshahi).
CORRECT ANSWER: False (Comilla is the one famously associated with Roshomalai. Rajshahi is famous for 'Roshokadam').
[MCQ] What is "Roshokadam"?
A) A type of mango
B) A traditional sweet of Rajshahi
C) A folk song
D) A river fish
CORRECT ANSWER: B) A traditional sweet of Rajshahi
[MCQ] The "Barendra" region has a distinct dialect and culture. It refers to...
A) The southern coastal area
B) The northern high land area (including Rajshahi)
C) The eastern hill tracts
D) The central plains
CORRECT ANSWER: B) The northern high land area (including Rajshahi)
[TRUE/FALSE] "Nakshi Kantha" (embroidered quilt) making is a common traditional craft in the rural areas of Rajshahi.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Kachagolla" is a famous sweet from which district in Rajshahi division?
A) Rajshahi
B) Natore
C) Pabna
D) Sirajganj
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Natore
[MCQ] The "Mask of Gambhira" is typically made of...
A) Metal
B) Wood (Neem tree)
C) Plastic
D) Clay
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Wood (Neem tree)
[TRUE/FALSE] "Doi" (Yogurt) of Bogra is famous all over Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Himsagar" and "Langra" are popular varieties of...
A) Litchi
B) Mango
C) Banana
D) Guava
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Mango
[MCQ] "Sirajganj" is famous for its traditional...
A) Handloom (Taant) industry
B) Pottery
C) Brass work
D) Jute crafts
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Handloom (Taant) industry
