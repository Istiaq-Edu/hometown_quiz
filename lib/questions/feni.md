=== FENI - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Feni is located in which administrative division?
A) Dhaka
B) Chittagong
C) Sylhet
D) Barisal
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Chittagong

[TRUE/FALSE] Feni is the gateway to Chittagong from Dhaka.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river flows through Feni?
A) Padma
B) Feni River and Muhuri River
C) Meghna
D) Jamuna
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Feni River and Muhuri River

[MCQ] "Muhuri Project" (Muhuri Irrigation Project) is a popular tourist spot in...
A) Feni Sadar
B) Parshuram
C) Sonagazi
D) Chhagalnaiya
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Sonagazi

[TRUE/FALSE] The Muhuri Project involves a dam on the Feni River.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Bijoy Singh Dighi" is a historic...
A) Pond/Lake associated with King Bijoy Singh
B) Fort
C) Mosque
D) Hill
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Pond/Lake

[MCQ] Feni shares a border with...
A) Myanmar
B) India (Tripura state)
C) Nepal
D) Bhutan
CORRECT ANSWER: B) India (Tripura state)

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Bilonia" land port is located in Feni.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Chhagalnaiya" and "Parshuram" are upazilas of...
A) Noakhali
B) Feni
C) Comilla
D) Chittagong
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Feni

[MCQ] The "Chandgazi Bhuiyan Mosque" is a Mughal-era mosque.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True
