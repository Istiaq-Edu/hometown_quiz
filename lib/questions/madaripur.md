=== MADARIPUR - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Madaripur is named after which Sufi saint?
A) Shah Jalal
B) Shah Madar (Badiuddin Shah Madar)
C) Lalon Shah
D) Hason Raja
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Shah Madar

[TRUE/FALSE] The shrine (dargah) of Shah Madar is located in Madaripur.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Madaripur is located in which administrative division?
A) Rajshahi
B) Dhaka
C) Khulna
D) Barisal
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Dhaka

[MCQ] Which river flows through Madaripur?
A) Jamuna
B) Padma and Arial Khan
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Padma and Arial Khan

[TRUE/FALSE] Madaripur is part of the greater Faridpur region historically.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Rajoir" upazila is known for...
A) Mountains
B) Agricultural production
C) Desert
D) Beach
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural production

[MCQ] Madaripur has...
A) Many hills
B) Flat terrain with rivers
C) Desert
D) Dense forests only
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Flat terrain with rivers

[TRUE/FALSE] The Padma River forms the northern boundary of Madaripur.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Shibchar" upazila is known for...
A) Hills
B) Agricultural production and ferry links
C) Tea gardens
D) Sea port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural production and ferry links

[MCQ] Madaripur has historic mosques and temples.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] River transport is important in Madaripur.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Kalkini" upazila is known for...
A) Mountains
B) Agricultural areas
C) Desert
D) Beach
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural areas

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] Madaripur is known for which traditional sweet?
A) Rosogolla
B) Khejur Gur (date palm jaggery) sweets
C) Chomchom
D) All types
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Khejur Gur sweets

[TRUE/FALSE] Date palm cultivation and jaggery production are common in Madaripur.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The dialect spoken in Madaripur is...
A) Sylheti
B) Standard Bengali with local variation
C) Chittagonian
D) Rangpuri
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Standard Bengali with local variation

[MCQ] "Pitha" and sweets are especially popular during...
A) Summer
B) Winter (with date palm jaggery)
C) Monsoon
D) Autumn
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Winter

[TRUE/FALSE] Fishing is an important occupation in Madaripur's rivers.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Traditional boat racing is popular in Madaripur.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] The main occupation in rural Madaripur is...
A) IT
B) Agriculture and fishing
C) Mining
D) Heavy industry
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agriculture and fishing

[TRUE/FALSE] "Nakshi Kantha" embroidery is practiced in rural Madaripur.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Everyday Bangladesh

[MCQ] The distance from Dhaka to Madaripur is approximately...
A) 50 km
B) 80 km

[MCQ] The Padma Bridge has improved connectivity to Madaripur from...
A) Chittagong
B) Dhaka

[TRUE/FALSE] Before the Padma Bridge, ferries were the main link to cross the river.

A) Dhaka Medical College
B) Madaripur Sadar Hospital
