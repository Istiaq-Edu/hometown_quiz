=== COMILLA - RE-CATEGORIZED AND EXPANDED QUIZ ===
Category: Places and History (Minimum 10 Questions)
This category focuses on specific landmarks, geographical features, historical events, and significant figures associated with Comilla's past and present.

[MCQ] Comilla was historically known as...
A) Tippera / Tripura
B) Samatata
C) Nasirabad
D) Jalalabad
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Tippera / Tripura (It was the district of Tippera).
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Shalban Vihara" in Mainamati, Comilla, is an ancient Buddhist archaeological site.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which river flows past Comilla?
A) Gumti
B) Padma
C) Jamuna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Gumti
[MCQ] The "Comilla Victoria College" is named after...
A) Queen Victoria
B) Victoria Memorial
C) A local landlord
D) A British Governor
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Queen Victoria
[MCQ] "BARD" (Bangladesh Academy for Rural Development) is located in Comilla. It was founded by...
A) Dr. Muhammad Yunus
B) Akhter Hameed Khan
C) Fazle Hasan Abed
D) Atiqul Islam
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Akhter Hameed Khan
[TRUE/FALSE] The "War Cemetery" in Mainamati, Comilla, contains graves of soldiers from World War II.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which famous musician and composer, known as the "Surer Rajkumar" (Prince of Melody), was from Comilla?
A) Sachin Dev Burman
B) Kazi Nazrul Islam
C) Rabindranath Tagore
D) Lalon Shah
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Sachin Dev Burman
[MCQ] The "Dharmasagar" is a large man-made pond in Comilla city. It was dug by...
A) King Dharma Manikya
B) Queen Victoria
C) Akhter Hameed Khan
D) Mughal Emperor Akbar
CORRECT ANSWER: A) King Dharma Manikya
[TRUE/FALSE] Comilla is a major transit point between Dhaka and Chittagong.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Kotbari" is famous for...
A) Archaeological sites (Mainamati)
B) Industrial zone
C) River port
D) Tea gardens
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Archaeological sites (Mainamati)
[MCQ] Which cadet college is located in Comilla?
A) Faujdarhat Cadet College
B) Comilla Cadet College
C) Mirzapur Cadet College
D) Rajshahi Cadet College
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Comilla Cadet College
[TRUE/FALSE] Nawab Faizunnesa Chowdhurani, a pioneer feminist and writer, was from Comilla (Laksam).
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] The "Itakhola Mura" is an archaeological site located in...
A) Mainamati
B) Laksam
C) Daudkandi
D) Chandina
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Mainamati
[MCQ] "Rupban Mura" is another famous site in Mainamati.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True
[MCQ] "Dhirendranath Datta", a key figure in the Language Movement who first proposed Bengali as a state language in the Pakistan Constituent Assembly, was from Comilla.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True
[MCQ] "Shib Narayan Das", the designer of the first flag of Bangladesh, was from Comilla.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True
[MCQ] "Magic Paradise Park" is a popular amusement park in...
A) Kotbari, Comilla
B) Dhaka
C) Chittagong
D) Sylhet
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Kotbari, Comilla
[TRUE/FALSE] "Lalmai Hills" are a range of low hills in Comilla.
CORRECT ANSWER: True


Category: Culture and Traditions (Minimum 10 Questions)
This category covers festivals, arts, crafts, specific cultural practices, and aspects that define the unique identity and heritage of Comilla and its people.

[MCQ] Comilla is most famous for which sweet?
A) Rosogolla
B) Roshomalai
C) Monda
D) Sandesh
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Roshomalai (Matri Bhandar is the most famous shop).
[TRUE/FALSE] "Khadi" or "Khaddar" cloth is a traditional heritage of Comilla.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Kazi Nazrul Islam, the National Poet, had a deep connection with Comilla and married...
A) Pramila Devi
B) Fazilatunnesa
C) Nargis
D) Both A and C (at different times/situations)
CORRECT ANSWER: D) Both A and C (He married Nargis in Daulatpur, Comilla, and later Pramila Devi from Comilla town).
[MCQ] The "Tripura" indigenous community lives in the border areas of Comilla.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True
[MCQ] Which pottery industry in Comilla is famous for its artistic value?
A) Bijoypur Pottery
B) Dhamrai Pottery
C) Rayer Bazar Pottery
D) Patuakhali Pottery
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Bijoypur Pottery
[TRUE/FALSE] Comilla has a strong tradition of "Jatra" (folk theatre).
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Raj Rajeshwari Kalibari" is a historic temple in Comilla.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] The "Comilla Cooperative Model" (BARD) is world-famous for...
A) Microfinance
B) Rural Development and Cooperatives
C) Industrialization
D) Education
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rural Development and Cooperatives
[TRUE/FALSE] "Pita-Puli" festivals are common in Comilla during harvest.

B) Durga Puja

[MCQ] "Comilla Club" is a historic social club.

Category: Everyday Bangladesh (Comilla Focus) (Minimum 10 Questions)
