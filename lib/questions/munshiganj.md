=== MUNSHIGANJ - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Munshiganj is known as the land of...
A) Mountains
B) Bikrampur (historic region)
C) Deserts
D) Tea gardens
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bikrampur (historic region)

[TRUE/FALSE] Bikrampur was a significant administrative and cultural center in ancient Bengal.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which famous scholar was born in Bikrampur (Munshiganj)?
A) Rabindranath Tagore
B) Atish Dipankar
C) Kazi Nazrul Islam
D) Lalon Shah
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Atish Dipankar

[MCQ] Atish Dipankar was a famous...
A) Musician
B) Buddhist scholar who spread Buddhism in Tibet
C) Politician
D) Cricketer
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Buddhist scholar

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Atish Dipankar" memorial is located in Munshiganj.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river flows near Munshiganj?
A) Jamuna
B) Padma, Meghna, and Dhaleshwari
C) Karnaphuli
D) Teesta
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Padma, Meghna, and Dhaleshwari

[MCQ] "Idrakpur Fort" is a historic...
A) Temple
B) Mughal-era fort
C) Palace
D) Mosque only
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Mughal-era fort

[TRUE/FALSE] Idrakpur Fort was built to defend against Magh and Portuguese pirates.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Munshiganj is located in which administrative division?
A) Rajshahi
B) Dhaka
C) Khulna
D) Chittagong
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Dhaka

[MCQ] "Mirkadim" area is known for...
A) Mountains
B) Historic sites and river activities
C) Desert
D) Tea
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Historic sites and river activities

[TRUE/FALSE] Munshiganj is close to Dhaka, making it easily accessible.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Bajrayogini" is an ancient...
A) River
B) Buddhist archaeological site
C) Market
D) Forest
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Buddhist archaeological site

[MCQ] "Sreenagar" upazila is known for...
A) Hills
B) Historic Bikrampur area

D) Sea port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Historic Bikrampur area

[TRUE/FALSE] Many ancient temples and archaeological sites are found in Munshiganj.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
