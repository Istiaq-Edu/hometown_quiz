=== BARGUNA - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Barguna is located in which administrative division?
A) Khulna
B) Barisal
C) Chittagong
D) Dhaka
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Barisal

[TRUE/FALSE] Barguna is a coastal district facing the Bay of Bengal.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which rivers flow through Barguna?
A) Padma
B) Payra, Bishkhali, and Baleshwar
C) Jamuna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Payra, Bishkhali, and Baleshwar

[MCQ] "Harinbaria" or "Tengragiri" is a...
A) Mangrove forest and eco-park
B) Mountain
C) Desert
D) Tea garden
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Mangrove forest and eco-park

[TRUE/FALSE] Tengragiri Eco Park is often called the "second Sundarbans" of the region.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Sonakata" is a known...
A) Tourist spot and beach
B) Hill
C) Fort
D) Airport
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Tourist spot and beach

[MCQ] "Bibi Chini Shahi Mosque" is a historic mosque in...
A) Betagi upazila
B) Patharghata
C) Amtali
D) Taltali
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Betagi upazila

[TRUE/FALSE] Barguna was historically part of the Sundarbans forest area.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Patharghata" upazila is famous for...
A) Hills
B) Fishing harbor and BFDC fish landing station
C) Tea gardens
D) Airport
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Fishing harbor

[MCQ] "Shubhondhya" sea beach is a newly popular spot in Barguna.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] Barguna's culture is heavily influenced by...
A) River and coastal life
B) Mountain life
C) Desert life
D) Urban life
CORRECT ANSWER: A) River and coastal life

[TRUE/FALSE] Hilsa (Ilish) fishing and trading is a major economic activity.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The Rakhine community also lives in parts of Barguna (Taltali).
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] "Shutki" (Dried fish) production is common in...
A) Patharghata and Taltali
B) Dhaka
C) Sylhet
D) Rangpur
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Patharghata and Taltali

[TRUE/FALSE] Traditional boat making is a profession here.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Everyday Bangladesh

[MCQ] The distance from Dhaka to Barguna is approximately...
A) 200 km
B) 350 km
C) 500 km
D) 600 km
CORRECT ANSWER: B) 350 km

[MCQ] Launch (river vessel) is a primary mode of transport to Dhaka.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] Road connectivity has improved due to...
A) Bridges like Padma and Payra
B) Tunnels
C) Cable cars
D) Underground train
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Bridges like Padma and Payra

[TRUE/FALSE] Barguna is vulnerable to cyclones.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The main economic activities in Barguna include...
A) IT only
B) Fishing, agriculture, and increasing tourism
C) Heavy industry
D) Gold mining
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Fishing, agriculture, and increasing tourism
