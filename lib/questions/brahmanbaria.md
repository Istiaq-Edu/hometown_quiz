=== BRAHMANBARIA - RE-CATEGORIZED AND EXPANDED QUIZ ===

Category: Places & History (Minimum 15 Questions)
Historical sites, landmarks, and the rich heritage of Brahmanbaria.

[MCQ] Brahmanbaria is famous for being the birthplace of which legendary musician?
A) Ravi Shankar
B) Ustad Alauddin Khan
C) Zakir Hussain
D) Bismillah Khan
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Ustad Alauddin Khan

[TRUE/FALSE] Ustad Alauddin Khan is known as the founder of the "Maihar Gharana" of Indian classical music.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Brahmanbaria is often called the...
A) City of Mosques
B) Cultural Capital of Bangladesh / City of Classical Music
C) Port City
D) Industrial Capital
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Cultural Capital of Bangladesh / City of Classical Music

[MCQ] The "Kala Bhavan" (Art House) in Brahmanbaria was established for...
A) Industrial training
B) Promoting arts and music
C) Religious education
D) Agricultural research
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Promoting arts and music

[TRUE/FALSE] Many renowned classical musicians have roots in Brahmanbaria.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Titas River" is closely associated with which district?
A) Comilla
B) Brahmanbaria
C) Sylhet
D) Dhaka
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Brahmanbaria

[MCQ] The "Titas" is famous for...
A) Being the deepest river
B) Its cultural significance and Adwaita Mallabarman's novel "Titash Ekti Nadir Naam"
C) Hydroelectric power
D) Gold mining
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Its cultural significance and Adwaita Mallabarman's novel "Titash Ekti Nadir Naam"

[TRUE/FALSE] The novel "Titash Ekti Nadir Naam" (A River Called Titas) depicts the life of fishermen on the Titas River.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Adwaita Mallabarman," the author of "Titash Ekti Nadir Naam," was from...
A) Dhaka
B) Brahmanbaria
C) Kolkata
D) Sylhet
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Brahmanbaria

[MCQ] The "Ariful Haq Choudhury (Hason Raja) was a famous...
A) Politician
B) Mystic poet and folk musician from Sylhet (related to the region)
C) Scientist
D) General
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Mystic poet and folk musician from Sylhet (related to the region)

[TRUE/FALSE] Brahmanbaria has a rich tradition of Baul and folk music.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Sarail" upazila is famous for...
A) Being the birthplace of Ustad Alauddin Khan
B) Hills
C) Tea gardens
D) Sea port
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Being the birthplace of Ustad Alauddin Khan

[MCQ] Which historic mosque is located in Brahmanbaria?
A) Star Mosque
B) Various historic mosques dating to Mughal era
C) Baitul Mukarram
D) None
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Various historic mosques dating to Mughal era

[TRUE/FALSE] Brahmanbaria was part of the greater Comilla (Tripura) region historically.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Akhaura" upazila is significant for...
A) Being a border crossing to India (Tripura)
B) Hills
C) Tea gardens
D) Sea port
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Being a border crossing to India (Tripura)

[MCQ] The "Birshreshtha Mustafiz" memorial is located in Brahmanbaria, honoring a...
A) Poet
B) Liberation War hero
C) Musician
D) Politician
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Liberation War hero
