=== JESSORE - RE-CATEGORIZED AND EXPANDED QUIZ ===

Category: Places & History (Minimum 15 Questions)
Historical sites, landmarks, and the rich heritage of Jessore.

[MCQ] Jessore is historically significant because it was the first district to be...
A) Formed during Mughal era
B) Liberated during the 1971 Liberation War
C) Connected by railway
D) Electrified
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Liberated during the 1971 Liberation War

[TRUE/FALSE] Jessore was liberated on December 6, 1971, making it the first district freed from Pakistani occupation.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Jessore Collectorate Building" is a historic structure from which era?
A) Mughal
B) British Colonial
C) Sultanate
D) Modern
CORRECT ANSWER: B) British Colonial

[MCQ] The "Michael Madhusudan Dutt" statue and memorial in Jessore honors which famous...
A) Politician
B) Poet and playwright
C) Singer
D) Freedom fighter
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Poet and playwright

[TRUE/FALSE] Michael Madhusudan Dutt, the father of modern Bengali poetry, was born in Sagardari, Jessore.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Sagardari" is the birthplace of Michael Madhusudan Dutt, located in which upazila?
A) Jessore Sadar
B) Keshabpur
C) Manirampur
D) Abhaynagar
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Keshabpur

[MCQ] The "Chanchra Shahi Mosque" is a historical mosque built during...
A) Mughal period
B) Sultanate period
C) British period
D) Pakistan period
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sultanate period

[TRUE/FALSE] Jessore was part of the ancient Sundarbans region before human settlement.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Jessore Road" is a famous...
A) Modern highway
B) Historic road connecting Kolkata to Dhaka
C) Railway line
D) River route
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Historic road connecting Kolkata to Dhaka

[MCQ] Which river flows through Jessore district?
A) Padma
B) Bhairab and Kabodak
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bhairab and Kabodak

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Noapara" area in Jessore is famous for its border crossing to India.
CORRECT ANSWER: False (Benapole is the famous land port)

[MCQ] The "Benapole" land port is the busiest...
A) Sea port
B) Land port between Bangladesh and India
C) Airport
D) River port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Land port between Bangladesh and India

[MCQ] The "Gazi Kalu Champion" is a legendary folk hero associated with the...
A) Hills
B) Sundarbans and southern Bengal
C) Northern plains
D) Eastern hills
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sundarbans and southern Bengal (worshipped in Jessore region)

[TRUE/FALSE] Jessore was an important center during the British Raj for indigo cultivation.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Jessore Cantonment" is located in which area?
A) Jessore Sadar
B) Jashore Sadar (near Noapara area)
C) Benapole
D) Jhenaidah
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Jashore Sadar (near Noapara area)

[MCQ] The "Sammilani" is a famous...
A) Temple
B) Cultural and social organization building in Jessore
C) Mosque
D) Stadium
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Cultural and social organization building in Jessore

[TRUE/FALSE] Jessore is now officially spelled "Jashore" in Bengali.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Culture & Traditions (Minimum 15 Questions)
Local customs, festivals, food, and the cultural identity of Jessore.

[MCQ] Jessore is nationally famous for which date product?
A) Date juice (Khejur Ras)
B) Date palm jaggery (Khejur Gur)
C) Date fruits
D) All of the above, especially Khejur Gur
CORRECT ANSWER: D) All of the above, especially Khejur Gur

[TRUE/FALSE] Jessore's date palm jaggery (Khejur Gur) is considered one of the best in Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Pitha Utsab" (Rice Cake Festival) is popular in Jessore during...
A) Summer
B) Winter (Poush month)
C) Monsoon
D) Autumn
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Winter (Poush month)

[MCQ] Which flower is Jessore famous for, being one of the major flower-producing districts?
A) Roses only
B) Gladiolus, Roses, and various flowers
C) Orchids only
D) Lotuses only
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Gladiolus, Roses, and various flowers

[TRUE/FALSE] Jessore is called the "Flower Capital" of Bangladesh due to its commercial flower cultivation.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Godkhali" area is famous for...
A) Fish market
B) Flower cultivation and market
C) Cattle market
D) Rice mill
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Flower cultivation and market

[MCQ] Which sweet is traditionally made with Jessore's date palm jaggery?
A) Rosogolla
B) Patishapta, Payesh, and various Pithas
C) Chomchom
D) Monda
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Patishapta, Payesh, and various Pithas

[TRUE/FALSE] "Nakshi Kantha" embroidery is also practiced in parts of Jessore.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Jhinger Kella" was a historic fort in Jessore used during...
A) Mughal era
B) British era
C) Liberation War of 1971
D) Sultanate era
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Liberation War of 1971

[MCQ] Which dialect/language variation is spoken in Jessore?
A) Standard Bengali similar to Kolkata Bengali
B) Sylheti
C) Chittagonian
D) Rangpuri
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Standard Bengali similar to Kolkata Bengali

[TRUE/FALSE] Fish, especially from the local rivers, is a major part of Jessore's cuisine.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Baul" and "Fakiri" songs are popular folk music forms in Jessore.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] The "Chitra River" was historically important for...
A) Gold mining
B) Trade and transportation
C) Hydropower
D) Fishing only
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Trade and transportation

[TRUE/FALSE] Jessore has a tradition of theater and drama, influenced by the Bengali cultural heritage.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which traditional industry was once prominent in Jessore but has declined?
A) Silk weaving
B) Indigo processing
C) Shipbuilding
D) Tea processing
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Indigo processing
