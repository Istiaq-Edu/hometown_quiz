=== CHANDPUR - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Chandpur is famously known as the...
A) City of Mosques
B) City of Hilsa (Ilish Er Bari)
C) City of Silk
D) Port City
CORRECT ANSWER: B) City of Hilsa (Ilish Er Bari)

[TRUE/FALSE] Chandpur is located at the confluence of the Padma, Meghna, and Dakatia rivers.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Molhead" (Mohona) in Chandpur is a popular...
A) Tourist spot to view the river confluence
B) Mountain
C) Deep forest
D) Historical fort
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Tourist spot to view the river confluence

[MCQ] Chandpur was historically part of which district?
A) Dhaka
B) Comilla
C) Noakhali
D) Barisal
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Comilla

[TRUE/FALSE] Chandpur is a major river port in Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river is NOT one of the rivers meeting at Chandpur?
A) Padma
B) Meghna
C) Dakatia
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: D) Karnaphuli

[MCQ] "Hajiganj" is famous for...
A) Hajiganj Bara Mosque (Great Mosque)
B) Tea gardens
C) Sea beach
D) Mountains
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Hajiganj Bara Mosque

[TRUE/FALSE] The Hajiganj Bara Mosque is one of the largest mosques in the subcontinent.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Matlab" upazila is well known for...
A) ICDDR,B Cholera Hospital and research
B) Tourism
C) Mining
D) Airport
CORRECT ANSWER: A) ICDDR,B Cholera Hospital and research

[MCQ] Chandpur is in which administrative division?
A) Dhaka
B) Chittagong
C) Barisal
D) Khulna
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Chittagong

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] The most famous food item from Chandpur is...
A) Mango
B) Hilsa Fish (Ilish)
C) Rosogolla
D) Tea
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Hilsa Fish (Ilish)

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Chandpur Launch Terminal" is one of the busiest river terminals.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Pitha" and sweets are popular in Chandpur.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] The people of Chandpur are known for their hospitality and affinity for...
A) River travel
B) Mountain trekking
C) Desert safaris
D) Snow skiing
CORRECT ANSWER: A) River travel

[TRUE/FALSE] Fishing is a major livelihood in Chandpur.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Three Rivers Meeting Point" is locally called...
A) Mohona
B) Sangam
C) Delta
D) Estuary
CORRECT ANSWER: a) Mohona

Category: Everyday Bangladesh

[MCQ] The commute between Dhaka and Chandpur is most popular via...
A) Bus
B) Launch (River vessel)
C) Train
D) Air
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Launch (River vessel)

[TRUE/FALSE] The launch journey from Dhaka to Chandpur takes about 3-4 hours.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The distance from Dhaka to Chandpur by road is approximately...
A) 100 km
B) 170 km
C) 250 km
D) 350 km
CORRECT ANSWER: B) 170 km

[MCQ] "Chandpur Government College" is a prominent educational institution.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[MCQ] The main economic activities in Chandpur include...
A) IT only
B) Fishing, trade (river port), and agriculture
C) Heavy industry
D) Mining
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Fishing, trade (river port), and agriculture

[TRUE/FALSE] Chandpur has rail connectivity to Chittagong.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
