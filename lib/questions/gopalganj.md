=== GOPALGANJ - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Gopalganj is the birthplace of which famous Bangladeshi leader?
A) Ziaur Rahman
B) Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman
C) Hussain Muhammad Ershad
D) Khaleda Zia
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman

[TRUE/FALSE] Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman is known as the "Father of the Nation" of Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Tungipara" is famous as the...
A) Capital of Gopalganj
B) Birthplace of Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman
C) Industrial zone
D) Beach resort
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Birthplace of Bangabandhu

[MCQ] The "Bangabandhu Mausoleum Complex" is located in...
A) Dhaka
B) Tungipara, Gopalganj
C) Chittagong
D) Sylhet
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Tungipara, Gopalganj

[TRUE/FALSE] Many visitors come to Gopalganj to pay tribute at Bangabandhu's mausoleum.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Gopalganj is located in which administrative division?
A) Rajshahi
B) Dhaka
C) Khulna
D) Barisal
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Dhaka

[MCQ] Which river flows through Gopalganj?
A) Jamuna
B) Madhumati
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Madhumati

[TRUE/FALSE] Gopalganj was part of greater Faridpur district.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Kotalipara" upazila is known for...
A) Mountains
B) Agricultural production
C) Desert
D) Beach
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural production

[MCQ] Gopalganj has...
A) Mountainous terrain
B) Low-lying wetland areas
C) Desert
D) Dense forests only
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Low-lying wetland areas

[TRUE/FALSE] Gopalganj experiences seasonal flooding.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Kashiani" upazila is known for...
A) Hills
B) Agricultural areas
C) Tea gardens
D) Sea port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agricultural areas

[MCQ] The "Ulpur" area is significant for...
A) Being the ancestral home of Bangabandhu's family
B) Mountains
C) Industrial zone
D) Airport
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Being the ancestral home

[TRUE/FALSE] Many historic sites related to Bangabandhu are found in Gopalganj.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

Category: Culture & Traditions

[MCQ] Gopalganj is known for which traditional sweet?
A) Rosogolla
B) Khejur Gur (date palm jaggery) sweets
C) Chomchom
D) All types
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Khejur Gur sweets

[TRUE/FALSE] Date palm cultivation and jaggery production are common in Gopalganj.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The dialect spoken in Gopalganj is...
A) Sylheti
B) Standard Bengali with local variation
C) Chittagonian
D) Rangpuri
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Standard Bengali with local variation

[MCQ] "Pitha" and sweets are especially popular during...
A) Summer
B) Winter (with date palm jaggery)
C) Monsoon
D) Autumn
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Winter

[TRUE/FALSE] Fishing is an important occupation in Gopalganj.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The main occupation in rural Gopalganj is...
A) IT
B) Agriculture and fishing
C) Mining
D) Heavy industry
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Agriculture and fishing

[MCQ] Traditional boat racing is popular in Gopalganj.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] "Nakshi Kantha" embroidery is practiced in rural Gopalganj.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
