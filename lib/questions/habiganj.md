=== HABIGANJ - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Habiganj is located in which administrative division?
A) Dhaka
B) Sylhet
C) Chittagong
D) Mymensingh
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sylhet

[TRUE/FALSE] Habiganj is part of the greater Sylhet region known for tea and natural gas.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which river flows through Habiganj district?
A) Padma
B) Kushiyara and Khowai
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Kushiyara and Khowai

[MCQ] The "Satchari National Park" is located in which upazila?
A) Habiganj Sadar
B) Chunarughat
C) Madhabpur
D) Nabiganj
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Chunarughat

[TRUE/FALSE] Satchari National Park is home to diverse wildlife including the Hoolock Gibbon.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Lawachara National Park" is located near which area?
A) Habiganj Sadar
B) Near Srimangal (Moulvibazar, but accessible from Habiganj)
C) Dhaka
D) Chittagong
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Near Srimangal

[MCQ] The "Rema-Kalenga Wildlife Sanctuary" is located in...
A) Habiganj Sadar
B) Chunarughat
C) Madhabpur
D) Nabiganj
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Chunarughat

[TRUE/FALSE] Habiganj has significant tea gardens.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which ancient archaeological site is associated with the Sylhet-Habiganj region?
A) Mahasthangarh
B) Various ancient mounds and historic sites
C) Paharpur
D) Mainamati
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Various ancient mounds and historic sites

[MCQ] "Lakhai" upazila is known for...
A) Hills
B) Haor (wetland) areas
C) Tea gardens
D) Sea port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Haor (wetland) areas

[TRUE/FALSE] Habiganj has both hilly and haor terrain.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Madhabpur Lake" is a scenic...
A) Sea
B) Lake surrounded by tea gardens
C) River
D) Desert oasis
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Lake surrounded by tea gardens

[MCQ] Gas fields are found in Habiganj district.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Habiganj Gas Field" is one of the major gas fields in Bangladesh.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Bahubal" upazila is known for...
A) Beach
B) Tea gardens and hills
C) Desert
D) Industrial zone
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Tea gardens and hills

[MCQ] The "Khoja Mosque" is a historic mosque in Habiganj from which era?
A) Sultanate
B) Mughal period
C) British
D) Modern
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Mughal period
