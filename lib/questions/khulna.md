=== KHULNA - RE-CATEGORIZED AND EXPANDED QUIZ ===
Category: Places and History (Minimum 10 Questions)
This category focuses on specific landmarks, geographical features, historical events, and significant figures associated with Khulna's past and present.

[MCQ] Khulna is known as the gateway to the...
A) Himalayas
B) Sundarbans
C) Tea Gardens
D) Hill Tracts
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Sundarbans
[TRUE/FALSE] The Sixty Dome Mosque (Shat Gombuj Masjid), a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is located in the Khulna Division (Bagerhat).
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Which river flows by the city of Khulna?
A) Rupsha
B) Padma
C) Meghna
D) Karnaphuli
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Rupsha
[MCQ] Who was the famous saint and ruler associated with the historic mosques in Bagerhat (near Khulna)?
A) Shah Jalal
B) Khan Jahan Ali
C) Shah Makhdum
D) Bayazid Bostami
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Khan Jahan Ali
[MCQ] The "Mongla Port", the second largest seaport in Bangladesh, is located in which division?
A) Chittagong
B) Barisal
C) Khulna
D) Dhaka
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Khulna
[TRUE/FALSE] The Sundarbans is the largest mangrove forest in the world.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] The "Khulna Shipyard" is a major industrial facility located on the banks of which river?
A) Bhairab
B) Rupsha
C) Kopotakkho
D) Pashur
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Rupsha (Technically Kazi Bacha/Rupsha).
[MCQ] Which famous poet was born in the village of Sagardari in the Khulna division (Jessore district)?
A) Rabindranath Tagore
B) Kazi Nazrul Islam
C) Michael Madhusudan Dutt
D) Jibanananda Das
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Michael Madhusudan Dutt
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Fultala" area in Khulna is known for its zoo.
CORRECT ANSWER: True (Jahanabad Cantonment Park/Zoo is nearby).
[MCQ] The "Dakshindih" old house is associated with the in-laws of which famous personality?
A) Rabindranath Tagore
B) Kazi Nazrul Islam
C) Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman
D) Lalon Shah
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Rabindranath Tagore
[MCQ] "Karamjal" is a popular tourist spot and breeding center for...
A) Tigers
B) Crocodiles and Deer
C) Elephants
D) Birds
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Crocodiles and Deer (in the Sundarbans).
[TRUE/FALSE] Khulna was a major center for jute industry in the past, often called the "Dundee of the East" (along with Narayanganj).
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Hiron Point" is a famous spot in the Sundarbans known for...
A) Tiger spotting
B) Beach activities
C) Mountain climbing
D) Shopping
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Tiger spotting (and wildlife viewing).
[MCQ] "Dublar Char" is an island in the Sundarbans famous for...
A) Dried fish processing and Rash Mela
B) Coconut trees
C) Tea gardens
D) Ship breaking
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Dried fish processing and Rash Mela
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Mujibnagar" memorial, where the first government of Bangladesh took oath, is located in the Khulna division (Meherpur).
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Kotka" Beach is a pristine beach located in...
A) Cox's Bazar
B) Kuakata
C) The Sundarbans (Khulna)
D) St. Martin's
CORRECT ANSWER: C) The Sundarbans (Khulna)
[MCQ] The "Shilaidaha Kuthibari", associated with Rabindranath Tagore, is located in which district of Khulna division?
A) Khulna
B) Kushtia
C) Jessore
D) Satkhira
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Kushtia
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Bhairab River" is one of the major rivers in the Khulna region.
CORRECT ANSWER: True


Category: Culture and Traditions (Minimum 10 Questions)
This category covers festivals, arts, crafts, specific cultural practices, and aspects that define the unique identity and heritage of Khulna and its people.

[MCQ] "Chui Jhal" is a famous spice/ingredient used in Khulna's cuisine, especially with beef or mutton. Which part of the plant is used?
A) Leaves
B) Seeds
C) Stem/Stalk
D) Flower
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Stem/Stalk
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Bonbibi" worship is a unique cultural practice among the people living near the Sundarbans, revered by both Muslims and Hindus.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Rash Mela" is a famous festival held in the Sundarbans area (Dublar Char). It is primarily a...
A) Muslim festival
B) Hindu festival
C) Buddhist festival
D) Christian festival
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Hindu festival
[MCQ] Which fish is most iconic to the Khulna region due to its shrimp farming industry?
A) Hilsa (Ilish)
B) Golda Chingri (Prawn) / Bagda Chingri (Shrimp)
C) Rui
D) Pangas
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Golda Chingri (Prawn) / Bagda Chingri (Shrimp)
[TRUE/FALSE] "Gazi'r Pot" is a traditional folk art form of storytelling with scroll paintings, popular in the Khulna region.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Lalon Shah, the great Baul saint, has his shrine in Kushtia, which is part of the...
A) Rajshahi Division
B) Khulna Division
C) Dhaka Division
D) Barisal Division
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Khulna Division
[MCQ] "Nakshi Kantha" of the Jessore/Khulna region is famous for its intricate...
A) Painting
B) Stitching/Embroidery
C) Woodwork
D) Metalwork
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Stitching/Embroidery
[TRUE/FALSE] Honey collection (Mouals) is a traditional and risky profession in the Sundarbans area of Khulna.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Sandesh" of Satkhira (Khulna Division) is famous for its...
A) Spiciness
B) Unique taste and quality
C) Sourness
D) Hard texture
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Unique taste and quality (Pranhara Sandesh etc).
[MCQ] The "Baul" music tradition is strongly associated with which district in the Khulna division?
A) Khulna
B) Bagerhat
C) Kushtia
D) Satkhira
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Kushtia
[TRUE/FALSE] "Pot Gaan" is a folk song genre popular in this region.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] "Date Molasses" (Khejur Gur) of Jessore is famous for its...
A) Color
B) Aroma and taste
C) Hardness
D) Saltiness
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Aroma and taste
[MCQ] "Kulfi Malai" of Kushtia is a popular...
A) Ice cream/Dessert
B) Spicy curry
C) Drink
D) Bread
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Ice cream/Dessert
[MCQ] The "Gombhira" festival is to Rajshahi as "______" is to the Sundarbans area (worship of forest deity).
A) Bonbibi Mela
B) Baul Mela
C) Lalon Mela
D) Poush Mela
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Bonbibi Mela
[TRUE/FALSE] "Tiler Khaja" is a famous sweet snack from Kushtia.
CORRECT ANSWER: True


Category: Everyday Bangladesh (Khulna Focus) (Minimum 10 Questions)
This category focuses on the practical, social, and logistical aspects of daily life in Khulna, including transport, demographics, administration, and modern infrastructure.

[MCQ] Khulna is the...
A) Largest city in Bangladesh
B) Second largest city in Bangladesh
C) Third largest city in Bangladesh
D) Fourth largest city in Bangladesh
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Third largest city in Bangladesh (after Dhaka and Chittagong).
[MCQ] The Khan Jahan Ali Airport is a proposed/under-construction airport in...
A) Jessore
B) Bagerhat (near Khulna)
C) Satkhira
D) Kushtia
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Bagerhat (near Khulna) - (Currently Jessore Airport serves Khulna).
[MCQ] Which university in Khulna is renowned for engineering and technology?
A) Khulna University (KU)
B) Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET)
C) Khulna Agricultural University
D) Northern University
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET)
[TRUE/FALSE] The "Padma Bridge" has significantly reduced travel time between Dhaka and Khulna.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] Khulna Railway Station is a terminal station. Which major train connects it to Dhaka?
A) Sundarban Express
B) Parabat Express
C) Turna Nishita
D) Subarna Express
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Sundarban Express (and Chitra Express).
[MCQ] The "Khulna Newsprint Mills" was a famous industrial unit located in...
A) Khalishpur
B) Sonadanga
C) Daulatpur
D) Rupsha
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Khalishpur
[TRUE/FALSE] Khulna has a divisional stadium named "Sheikh Abu Naser Stadium" which hosts international cricket.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] What is the main source of water supply for Khulna city?
A) Groundwater only
B) Madhumati River
C) Surface water treatment (from Modhumoti/Rupsha) and groundwater
D) Rainwater harvesting
CORRECT ANSWER: C) Surface water treatment (from Modhumoti/Rupsha) and groundwater (KWASA projects).
[MCQ] Which bridge connects Khulna city with the Rupsha upazila and Mongla port road?
A) Khan Jahan Ali Bridge (Rupsha Bridge)
B) Lalon Shah Bridge
C) Hardinge Bridge
D) Bhairab Bridge
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Khan Jahan Ali Bridge (Rupsha Bridge)
[TRUE/FALSE] Shrimp farming (Gher) is a major economic activity in the rural areas surrounding Khulna.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
[MCQ] The "Khulna University" is unique in Bangladesh for not having...
A) Student politics
B) Tuition fees
C) Hostels
D) Exams
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Student politics
[MCQ] "Benapole" land port, the largest in Bangladesh, is located in which district of Khulna division?
A) Khulna
B) Jessore
C) Satkhira
D) Kushtia
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Jessore
[MCQ] The "Jessore IT Park" (Sheikh Hasina Software Technology Park) is a major tech hub in the region.
A) True
B) False
CORRECT ANSWER: A) True
[MCQ] "Sonadanga" is known as the central...
A) Bus Terminal area
B) Railway Station area
C) Launch Terminal area
D) Airport area
CORRECT ANSWER: A) Bus Terminal area
[TRUE/FALSE] Salinity intrusion is a major environmental issue in the coastal areas of Khulna.
CORRECT ANSWER: True
