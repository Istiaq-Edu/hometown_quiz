=== NETROKONA - QUIZ DATA ===

Category: Places & History

[MCQ] Netrokona is located in which administrative division?
A) Dhaka
B) Mymensingh
C) Sylhet
D) Rangpur
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Mymensingh

[TRUE/FALSE] Netrokona was part of greater Mymensingh district before becoming a separate district.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] Which hill range borders the northern part of Netrokona?
A) Chittagong Hill Tracts
B) Garo Hills (Meghalaya, India)
C) Sylhet Hills
D) Madhupur Garh
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Garo Hills (Meghalaya, India)

[MCQ] The "Susang Durgapur" area is known for its...
A) Industrial zone
B) Scenic beauty and proximity to Garo Hills
C) Sea port
D) Airport
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Scenic beauty and proximity to Garo Hills

[TRUE/FALSE] Netrokona has areas with hilly terrain near the Indian border.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Birishiri" is a famous...
A) River
B) Scenic tourist spot near Garo Hills
C) Industrial zone
D) Port city
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Scenic tourist spot near Garo Hills

[MCQ] The "Rakhalgari" and "China Mati Pahar" (White Clay Hill) are located in...
A) Netrokona Sadar
B) Durgapur
C) Kendua
D) Mohanganj
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Durgapur

[TRUE/FALSE] The "Someshwari River" flows through Netrokona.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] "Kendua" upazila is associated with which famous musician?
A) Ravi Shankar
B) Ustad Alauddin Khan (birthplace nearby/associated)
C) Zakir Hussain
D) None
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Ustad Alauddin Khan

[MCQ] The "Atpara" upazila is known for...
A) Hills
B) Haor (wetland) areas
C) Tea gardens
D) Sea port
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Haor (wetland) areas

[TRUE/FALSE] Netrokona has significant haor (wetland) areas.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Mohanganj" upazila is known for...
A) Mountains
B) Haor and wetlands
C) Desert
D) Beach
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Haor and wetlands

[MCQ] Which indigenous community lives in parts of Netrokona?
A) Chakma
B) Garo and Hajong
C) Marma
D) Tripura
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Garo and Hajong

[TRUE/FALSE] The Hajong community has a presence in Netrokona.
CORRECT ANSWER: True

[MCQ] The "Komola Rani Dighi" is a historic...
A) Temple
B) Large pond/tank
C) Mountain
D) River
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Large pond/tank

[MCQ] Netrokona played a role in the 1971 Liberation War with...
A) Naval battles
B) Guerrilla operations in hilly and haor areas
C) Air strikes
D) None
CORRECT ANSWER: B) Guerrilla operations
